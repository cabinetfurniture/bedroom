Have questions about caring for your garden furniture? We've got you covered. Here are some frequently asked questions and answers to help you keep your outdoor oasis looking its best.

Question 1: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 1: To maintain its beauty and longevity, clean your garden furniture regularly. During the summer months, aim to clean it every 2-3 weeks. In the winter, monthly cleaning should suffice.

Question 2: What's the best way to clean garden furniture?
Answer 2: Use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth to gently clean your garden furniture. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as they can damage the finish.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 3: Invest in custom-fitted covers to safeguard your garden furniture from rain, sun, and dust. These covers will help extend the life of your furniture and keep it looking its best.

Question 4: What should I do if my garden furniture gets rusty?
Answer 4: Remove rust with a wire brush or sandpaper. Apply a rust-resistant primer and paint to prevent future rust formation.

Question 5: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer 5: Add cushions and pillows to your garden furniture for extra comfort and style. Choose fabrics that are weather-resistant and easy to clean.

Question 6: How do I store my garden furniture during the winter?
Answer 6: Clean and dry your garden furniture thoroughly before storing it. Choose a dry, well-ventilated area to store your furniture and cover it with a protective cover.
