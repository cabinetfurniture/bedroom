FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got answers!

Question 1: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?
Answer: When selecting garden furniture, consider factors like durability, weather resistance, comfort, style, and maintenance requirements. Choose materials that can withstand outdoor conditions and furniture with comfortable cushions and ergonomic designs.

Question 2: What are the different types of garden furniture materials?
Answer: Common materials include wood (teak, eucalyptus), metal (aluminum, wrought iron), wicker (natural, synthetic), and plastic (resin, PVC). Each material has unique properties, such as durability, weather resistance, and maintenance requirements.

Question 3: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer: Add comfortable cushions, outdoor rugs, and throws to enhance the comfort of your garden furniture. Use weather-resistant cushions that can withstand outdoor conditions.

Question 4: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: Use covers or store furniture indoors during harsh weather. Apply protective treatments or sealants to wood and metal furniture to prevent damage from moisture and UV rays.

Question 5: How can I create a cohesive look for my garden furniture?
Answer: Choose furniture pieces from the same collection or with similar designs and colors. Add outdoor accessories like cushions, throws, and plants to tie the look together.

Question 6: Where can I find quality garden furniture in Coleraine?
Answer: Visit Coleraine's reputable furniture shops that specialize in outdoor furniture. Look for shops with knowledgeable staff who can guide you in selecting the best furniture for your needs.

Question 7: How can I care for my garden furniture to extend its lifespan?
Answer: Regularly clean your furniture with mild soap and water. Apply protective treatments or sealants to wood and metal furniture as needed. Store furniture indoors or use covers during harsh weather to prevent damage.
