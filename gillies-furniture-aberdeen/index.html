FAQ

Garden furniture FAQs

Question 1: What materials are commonly used for garden furniture?
Answer: Garden furniture is typically made from materials that can withstand outdoor conditions, such as weather-resistant woods, metal (such as aluminum or wrought iron), plastic (such as polyethylene or PVC), and wicker (made from natural or synthetic fibers).

Question 2: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?
Answer: When selecting garden furniture, consider factors such as the size and layout of your outdoor space, the intended use of the furniture, the durability and weather resistance of the materials, and the overall style and aesthetics you want to achieve.

Question 3: How can I maintain my garden furniture properly?
Answer: Regular maintenance can extend the lifespan of your garden furniture. Clean the furniture periodically using a mild detergent and water, and dry it thoroughly. Apply protective coatings or sealants to wooden or metal furniture as recommended by the manufacturer.

Question 4: What are some tips for creating a cozy and inviting outdoor space with garden furniture?
Answer: To create a cozy and inviting outdoor space, arrange your garden furniture in a comfortable and functional layout. Add cushions, throws, and pillows for extra comfort and style. Incorporate outdoor rugs, lanterns, and potted plants to enhance the ambiance.

Question 5: How can I protect my garden furniture during harsh weather conditions?
Answer: During harsh weather conditions, consider using protective covers or storing your garden furniture in a sheltered area. If possible, bring cushions and other fabric items indoors to prevent damage from rain or snow.

Question 6: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture at reasonable prices?
Answer: You can find a wide selection of high-quality garden furniture at reasonable prices at Gillies Furniture Aberdeen. Their knowledgeable staff can assist you in choosing the perfect pieces for your outdoor space and provide expert advice on care and maintenance.
