Premium materials

Luxury conservatory furniture is crafted from the finest materials, ensuring both durability and aesthetic appeal. These materials are carefully selected for their ability to withstand the unique demands of conservatory living, such as exposure to sunlight, moisture, and temperature fluctuations.

Teak wood: Known for its exceptional durability and resistance to rot, teak is a popular choice for luxury conservatory furniture. Its rich golden-brown hue adds a touch of warmth and elegance to any space.

Wicker and rattan: These natural materials offer a combination of strength and flexibility, making them ideal for creating intricate designs. Wicker and rattan furniture is lightweight and airy, adding a touch of bohemian charm to your conservatory.

Aluminum: Lightweight and rust-resistant, aluminum is a great choice for conservatory furniture that will be exposed to the elements. It can be powder-coated in a variety of colors to match your dÃ©cor.
