Non-toxic finishes and paints

When choosing nursery furniture, it's important to make sure that the finishes and paints are non-toxic. This is especially important for pieces that your child will be chewing on or sucking on, such as cribs, changing tables, and toys.

Greenguard certification means that the furniture has been tested and found to meet strict standards for low chemical emissions.

By choosing nursery furniture with non-toxic finishes and paints, you can help to protect your child's health and well-being.

Here are some tips for choosing nursery furniture with non-toxic finishes and paints:

  • Look for furniture that is made from solid wood or other natural materials.

  • Choose furniture that has been finished with water-based paints or finishes.
  • Avoid furniture that has been finished with oil-based paints or finishes, as these can emit harmful fumes.
  • Look for furniture that has been Greenguard certified.
