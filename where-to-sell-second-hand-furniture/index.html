Where to Sell Second Hand Furniture

Written by Obama May 15, 2025 Â· 11 min read

Are you looking to declutter your home and make some extra cash? Selling your gently used furniture is a great way to do both. But where's the best place to sell it? There are a lot of options out there, but not all of them are created equal. In this article, we'll help you sort through the clutter and find the best place to sell your used furniture.

When choosing a place to sell your furniture, there are a few things you'll want to keep in mind: the price you're willing to accept, the amount of time and effort you're willing to put in, and the type of furniture you're selling. If you're looking to get the most money for your furniture, you'll probably want to sell it online or through a consignment shop. However, if you're looking for a quick and easy way to get rid of your furniture, you may want to consider selling it at a garage sale or donating it to charity.

Now that you know a little bit more about the different options available to you, it's time to start thinking about where you're going to sell your furniture. Here are a few of the most popular options:

where to sell second hand furniture

There are many options available when it comes to selling your second hand furniture. Whether you want to declutter your home, or make some extra cash, or both, there's a marketplace for you.

  • Online Marketplaces
  • Consignment Shops
  • Garage Sales
  • Thrift Stores
  • Auctions

Each option has its own set of pros and cons, so it's important to do your research and choose the one that's right for you.

Online Marketplaces

Online marketplaces are a great option for selling second hand furniture because they offer a wide reach and the potential to get a good price for your items. However, it's important to do your research and choose the right marketplace for your needs.

Some of the most popular online marketplaces for selling second hand furniture include:

  • Facebook Marketplace: This is a great option for selling furniture locally. You can create a listing for your item and then share it with friends and family in your area.
  • Craigslist: This is another popular option for selling furniture locally. However, it's important to be aware of the potential for scams on Craigslist.
  • eBay: This is a good option for selling furniture that is in good condition and that you think will appeal to a wider audience. However, eBay does charge listing fees, so it's important to factor that into your pricing.
  • Chairish: This is a high-end online marketplace for selling vintage and antique furniture. If you have high-quality furniture that you think will appeal to a discerning audience, Chairish is a good option.

When selling furniture online, it's important to take clear and well-lit photos of your items and to write a detailed description. You should also be prepared to answer questions from potential buyers.

Consignment Shops

Consignment shops are another great option for selling second hand furniture. They typically take a percentage of the sale price, so it's important to factor that into your pricing. However, consignment shops can be a good option if you don't want to deal with the hassle of selling your furniture yourself.

  • Pros:

    - Consignment shops can be a good option if you don't want to deal with the hassle of selling your furniture yourself.
    - They can also be a good option if you have high-quality furniture that you think will appeal to a discerning audience.

  • Cons:

    - Consignment shops typically take a percentage of the sale price, so it's important to factor that into your pricing.
    - It can also take some time for your furniture to sell, so you need to be patient.

  • Tips for selling furniture through a consignment shop:

    - Make sure your furniture is clean and in good condition.
    - Take clear and well-lit photos of your items.
    - Write a detailed description of your items, including the dimensions and any unique features.
    - Be prepared to answer questions from the consignment shop staff about your items.

  • How to find a consignment shop:

    - Ask friends and family for recommendations.
    - Search online for consignment shops in your area.
    - Visit different consignment shops to compare their rates and selection.

Once you've found a consignment shop that you're happy with, you can drop off your furniture and they will take care of the rest. They will typically clean and stage your furniture, and then they will list it for sale. When your furniture sells, you will receive a percentage of the sale price.

Garage Sales

Garage sales are a great way to sell second hand furniture if you want to get rid of it quickly and easily. However, it's important to keep in mind that you're unlikely to get as much money for your furniture as you would if you sold it online or through a consignment shop.

To have a successful garage sale, you'll need to do some planning and preparation. First, you'll need to gather all of the furniture that you want to sell. Then, you'll need to clean and stage your furniture so that it looks its best.

On the day of your garage sale, you'll need to set up a table or display area for your furniture. You'll also need to have a way to accept payment, such as cash, Venmo, or PayPal.

Here are some tips for having a successful garage sale:

  • Advertise your garage sale in advance. You can do this by posting flyers in your neighborhood, posting on social media, or placing an ad in your local newspaper.
  • Price your furniture fairly. You want to price your furniture so that it's attractive to buyers, but you also don't want to price it so low that you're losing money.
  • Be prepared to negotiate. Most buyers will expect to be able to negotiate the price of your furniture, so be prepared to come down on your prices a bit.
  • Be friendly and helpful. Potential buyers are more likely to buy from you if they feel like you're being friendly and helpful.

Thrift Stores

Thrift stores are another option for selling second hand furniture. They typically sell donated items, so you won't get as much money for your furniture as you would if you sold it online or through a consignment shop. However, thrift stores can be a good option if you want to get rid of your furniture quickly and easily.

To sell furniture to a thrift store, you'll need to contact the store and ask if they are accepting donations. If they are, you'll need to bring your furniture to the store and donate it. The store will then sell your furniture and use the proceeds to support their charitable programs.

Here are some tips for selling furniture to a thrift store:

  • Make sure your furniture is clean and in good condition. Thrift stores typically only accept furniture that is in good condition and that is clean.
  • Be prepared to donate your furniture. When you donate furniture to a thrift store, you are giving it away. You will not receive any money for your furniture.
  • Get a receipt for your donation. This will help you prove that you donated your furniture to a charitable organization, which may be helpful for tax purposes.

Auctions

Auctions are another option for selling second hand furniture. However, it's important to keep in mind that auctions can be risky. If your furniture doesn't sell, you won't get any money for it. Additionally, auction houses typically charge a commission on the sale of your furniture.

  • Pros:

    - Auctions can be a good option if you have high-quality furniture that you think will appeal to a discerning audience.
    - Auctions can also be a good option if you want to get rid of your furniture quickly and easily.

  • Cons:

    - Auctions can be risky. If your furniture doesn't sell, you won't get any money for it.
    - Auction houses typically charge a commission on the sale of your furniture.

  • Tips for selling furniture at auction:

    - Make sure your furniture is clean and in good condition.
    - Take clear and well-lit photos of your items.
    - Write a detailed description of your items, including the dimensions and any unique features.
    - Set a reserve price for your furniture. This is the minimum price that you are willing to accept for your furniture.

  • How to find an auction house:

    - Ask friends and family for recommendations.
    - Search online for auction houses in your area.
    - Visit different auction houses to compare their rates and selection.

Once you've found an auction house that you're happy with, you can consign your furniture to the auction. The auction house will then sell your furniture on your behalf. If your furniture sells, you will receive a percentage of the sale price.
