Easy to care for

Rattan furniture is easy to care for. It is resistant to water and mildew, so you can simply wipe it down with a damp cloth to clean it. You should avoid using harsh chemicals or cleaners on your rattan furniture, as this could damage the finish.

  • Wipe down with a damp cloth

    To clean your rattan furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth. You can use a mild soap solution if necessary. Rinse the furniture with clean water and dry it thoroughly with a clean cloth.

  • Vacuum or dust regularly

    To prevent dust and dirt from building up on your rattan furniture, vacuum or dust it regularly. You can use a soft brush attachment on your vacuum cleaner to avoid damaging the rattan.

  • Avoid harsh chemicals and cleaners

    Avoid using harsh chemicals or cleaners on your rattan furniture, as this could damage the finish. If you need to remove a stain, test a small area first with a mild soap solution. Rinse the area with clean water and dry it thoroughly with a clean cloth.

  • Store in a dry place

    When not in use, store your rattan furniture in a dry place. This will help to prevent the rattan from becoming damaged by moisture.

By following these simple care tips, you can keep your rattan furniture looking its best for many years to come.
