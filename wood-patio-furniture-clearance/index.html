Consider the quality of the materials.

When shopping for wood patio furniture clearance, it is important to consider the quality of the materials. Not all wood is created equal, and some types of wood are more durable than others. If you live in a climate with extreme weather conditions, you will want to choose furniture made from a durable wood, such as teak or redwood.

Here are some of the most common types of wood used for patio furniture:

  • Teak. Teak is a very durable wood that is resistant to rot and decay. It is also naturally water-resistant, making it a good choice for outdoor furniture.
  • Redwood. Redwood is another durable wood that is resistant to rot and decay. It is also naturally fire-resistant, making it a good choice for areas that are prone to wildfires.
  • Cedar. Cedar is a softwood that is known for its natural resistance to insects and decay. It is also a relatively inexpensive wood, making it a good value for your money.
  • Pine. Pine is a softwood that is not as durable as teak or redwood. However, it is a relatively inexpensive wood, making it a good choice for budget-conscious shoppers.

In addition to the type of wood, you should also consider the quality of the construction. Look for furniture that is well-made and has sturdy joints. Avoid furniture that is made from cheap materials or that has poor construction.

By considering the quality of the materials and construction, you can choose wood patio furniture that will last for many years to come.
