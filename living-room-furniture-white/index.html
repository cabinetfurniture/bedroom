  • Solid wood: Solid wood is one of the most durable materials for furniture. It is strong and resistant to scratches and stains.

  • Research the cost of different types of furniture. Get an idea of the average price range for the type of furniture you want.
  • Set a budget and stick to it. Once you've set a budget, stick to it as much as possible. Don't be tempted to overspend just because you find a piece of furniture that you love.
