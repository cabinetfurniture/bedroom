Nursery Wooden Furniture A Guide to Comfort and Style

Written by Petter Apr 02, 2025 · 13 min read

Nursery furniture is an essential part of creating a comfortable and inviting space for your little one. Wooden furniture is a popular choice for nurseries due to its durability, style, and safety. In this article, we will explore the benefits of using nursery wooden furniture and provide tips on choosing the right pieces for your child's room.

Wooden furniture is durable and long-lasting, making it a worthwhile investment for your child's growing needs. With proper care and maintenance, wooden furniture can be passed down through generations, creating a cherished heirloom for your family.

Now that we've covered the benefits of wooden furniture, let's dive into the details of choosing the right pieces for your nursery.

Nursery Wooden Furniture: 8 Key Features

When choosing nursery wooden furniture, there are several important points to consider to ensure the safety and comfort of your child. Here are 8 key features to look for:

  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Stylish and versatile
  • Safe and non-toxic
  • Easy to clean and maintain
  • Ergonomic and comfortable
  • Adjustable to grow with your child
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable
  • Certified by reputable safety standards

By considering these factors, you can choose nursery wooden furniture that will provide a safe, comfortable, and stylish environment for your little one.

Durable and long-lasting

Durability is a key consideration when choosing nursery furniture, as you want pieces that will withstand the wear and tear of everyday use. Wooden furniture is a great choice in this regard, as it is naturally strong and durable.

When choosing wooden furniture for your nursery, look for pieces that are made from solid wood, rather than particle board or MDF. Solid wood is more durable and will last longer, even with regular use. You should also consider the type of wood used, as some woods are more durable than others. Oak, maple, and cherry are all good choices for nursery furniture, as they are hard woods that are resistant to scratches and dents.

In addition to the type of wood, the construction of the furniture is also important. Look for pieces that are well-made and sturdy, with strong joints and solid hardware. Avoid furniture with sharp edges or corners, as these can be dangerous for children.

With proper care and maintenance, wooden nursery furniture can last for many years. Be sure to clean it regularly with a damp cloth and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasives. You should also avoid placing wooden furniture in direct sunlight, as this can cause the wood to fade or warp.

By choosing durable and long-lasting wooden nursery furniture, you can create a safe and comfortable space for your child that will last for years to come.

Stylish and versatile

In addition to being durable and long-lasting, nursery wooden furniture should also be stylish and versatile. You want pieces that will complement your child's room décor and that can grow with your child as they get older.

  • Neutral colors and finishes

    Nursery wooden furniture is available in a wide range of neutral colors and finishes, making it easy to find pieces that will match any décor. Neutral colors, such as white, cream, and beige, are a good choice for nurseries, as they create a calming and serene atmosphere. You can also find wooden furniture in more colorful finishes, such as pink, blue, and green, if you want to add a pop of color to your child's room.

  • Convertible pieces

    Many nursery wooden furniture pieces are convertible, meaning that they can be used for multiple purposes as your child grows. For example, a convertible crib can be converted into a toddler bed and then into a full-size bed. Convertible pieces are a great way to save money and space, and they can help you create a nursery that will grow with your child.

  • Timeless designs

    When choosing nursery wooden furniture, it is important to choose pieces that have timeless designs. This means choosing pieces that are not too trendy or dated. Timeless designs will never go out of style, and they can be used for multiple children.

  • Mix and match

    Nursery wooden furniture is versatile and can be mixed and matched to create a unique look for your child's room. Don't be afraid to mix and match different pieces from different collections or even different stores. You can create a truly unique and personal space for your child by mixing and matching different styles and finishes.

By choosing stylish and versatile nursery wooden furniture, you can create a space that is both beautiful and functional. You can find pieces that will complement your child's room décor and that can grow with your child as they get older.

Safe and non-toxic

When choosing nursery wooden furniture, it is important to choose pieces that are safe and non-toxic. You want to avoid furniture that is made with materials that could be harmful to your child.

  • Solid wood construction

    Solid wood furniture is the safest choice for nurseries, as it is free of harmful chemicals and VOCs (volatile organic compounds). Solid wood furniture is also more durable and long-lasting than furniture made from other materials, such as particle board or MDF.

  • Non-toxic finishes

    The finishes used on nursery wooden furniture should also be non-toxic. Avoid furniture that is finished with lead-based paint or other harmful chemicals. Look for furniture that is finished with natural, non-toxic finishes, such as beeswax or linseed oil.

  • Safety certifications

    When choosing nursery wooden furniture, look for pieces that are certified by a reputable safety organization, such as the Juvenile Products Manufacturers Association (JPMA). JPMA-certified furniture meets strict safety standards, ensuring that it is safe for use in nurseries.

  • Regular cleaning

    To keep your nursery wooden furniture safe and non-toxic, it is important to clean it regularly. Wipe down furniture with a damp cloth and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasives. You should also avoid placing wooden furniture in direct sunlight, as this can cause the wood to fade or warp.

By choosing safe and non-toxic nursery wooden furniture, you can create a healthy and safe environment for your child.

Easy to clean and maintain

Nursery wooden furniture should be easy to clean and maintain. You want pieces that can be wiped down quickly and easily, without the need for harsh chemicals or abrasives.

Here are a few tips for cleaning and maintaining nursery wooden furniture:

  • Wipe down furniture with a damp cloth regularly. This will remove dust and dirt and help to keep the furniture looking its best.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasives on wooden furniture. These can damage the finish and make the wood more susceptible to stains and scratches.
  • If you spill something on wooden furniture, blot it up immediately with a clean cloth. Do not rub, as this can spread the spill and make it more difficult to remove.
  • Avoid placing wooden furniture in direct sunlight, as this can cause the wood to fade or warp.
  • If your wooden furniture starts to look dull, you can apply a natural wood polish or beeswax to help restore its shine.

By following these tips, you can keep your nursery wooden furniture looking its best for years to come.

Ergonomic and comfortable

Nursery wooden furniture should be ergonomic and comfortable for both you and your child. You want pieces that are designed to support your child's body and that are comfortable to use.

  • Adjustable furniture

    Adjustable furniture is a great choice for nurseries, as it can be customized to fit your child's needs. Look for furniture that has adjustable heights and angles, so that you can find the perfect position for your child.

  • Supportive seating

    The seating in your nursery should be supportive and comfortable for your child. Look for chairs and sofas with good lumbar support and that are made from soft, durable materials.

  • Comfortable sleeping arrangements

    Your child's bed should be comfortable and supportive. Look for a mattress that is firm enough to support your child's body, but soft enough to be comfortable. You should also choose a bed that is the right size for your child.

  • Safe and secure

    All nursery furniture should be safe and secure for your child. Look for furniture that is sturdy and well-made, and that does not have any sharp edges or corners. You should also make sure that all furniture is anchored to the wall to prevent it from tipping over.

By choosing ergonomic and comfortable nursery wooden furniture, you can create a space that is both safe and comfortable for your child.

Adjustable to grow with your child

As your child grows, their needs will change. That's why it's important to choose nursery wooden furniture that is adjustable and can grow with your child.

Here are a few examples of adjustable nursery wooden furniture:

  • Convertible cribs can be converted into toddler beds and then into full-size beds. This is a great way to save money and space, and it ensures that your child will have a bed that fits their needs as they grow.
  • Adjustable changing tables can be raised and lowered to accommodate your child's height. This makes it easier to change your child's diaper, and it can also be used as a regular table once your child is older.
  • Adjustable high chairs can be raised and lowered to accommodate your child's height and weight. This makes it easier to feed your child, and it can also be used as a regular chair once your child is older.

By choosing adjustable nursery wooden furniture, you can create a space that will grow with your child and meet their changing needs.

Eco-friendly and sustainable

When choosing nursery wooden furniture, it is important to consider the environmental impact of your choices. Look for furniture that is made from sustainable materials and that is produced in an eco-friendly way.

  • Sustainably harvested wood

    Look for furniture that is made from wood that has been sustainably harvested. This means that the wood has been harvested from forests that are managed in a way that ensures their long-term health and viability.

  • Low-VOC finishes

    Choose furniture that is finished with low-VOC (volatile organic compound) finishes. VOCs are harmful chemicals that can be released into the air, contributing to indoor air pollution. Low-VOC finishes are better for the environment and for your child's health.

  • Recyclable materials

    Look for furniture that is made from recyclable materials. This will help to reduce the amount of waste that goes to landfills.

  • Energy-efficient production

    Choose furniture that is produced in an energy-efficient way. This will help to reduce the environmental impact of the manufacturing process.

By choosing eco-friendly and sustainable nursery wooden furniture, you can create a healthy and sustainable environment for your child.

Certified by reputable safety standards

When choosing nursery wooden furniture, it is important to look for pieces that are certified by reputable safety standards. This ensures that the furniture has been tested and meets strict safety requirements.

  • Juvenile Products Manufacturers Association (JPMA)

    The JPMA is a trade association that represents manufacturers of juvenile products, including nursery furniture. JPMA-certified furniture meets strict safety standards, including those for lead content, durability, and stability.

  • American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM)

    ASTM is an international standards organization that develops and publishes voluntary consensus standards for a wide range of products and materials, including nursery furniture. ASTM-certified furniture meets strict safety standards, including those for lead content, durability, and stability.

  • California Air Resources Board (CARB)

    CARB is a state agency that regulates air quality in California. CARB-certified furniture meets strict standards for VOC (volatile organic compound) emissions. VOCs are harmful chemicals that can be released into the air, contributing to indoor air pollution.

  • Forest Stewardship Council (FSC)

    The FSC is a non-profit organization that promotes responsible forest management. FSC-certified furniture is made from wood that has been harvested from forests that are managed in a sustainable way.

By choosing nursery wooden furniture that is certified by reputable safety standards, you can be sure that you are creating a safe and healthy environment for your child.
