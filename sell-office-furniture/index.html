Sell Your Office Furniture for Cash

Written by Daniel May 04, 2025 · 11 min read

Are you looking to declutter your workspace and get some extra cash? Selling your old office furniture is a great way to do both. But before you start listing your items online or calling around to used furniture stores, there are a few things you need to do to prepare.

First, take some time to clean and repair your furniture. This will make it more appealing to potential buyers and help you get a better price. If there are any major repairs that need to be made, you may want to consider having them done before you list the furniture for sale. You can also improve the look of your furniture by reupholstering it or adding new hardware.

Once your furniture is ready to sell, you need to decide where you want to list it. There are a number of online marketplaces where you can sell used office furniture, such as Craigslist, eBay, and Facebook Marketplace. You can also sell your furniture through local consignment stores or used furniture stores.

Clean and repair your furniture.

Before you list your office furniture for sale, it's important to clean and repair it. This will make your furniture more appealing to potential buyers and help you get a better price.

  • Clean your furniture thoroughly.

    Use a mild soap and water solution to clean your furniture. Be sure to get into all the nooks and crannies, and don't forget to clean the underside of the furniture.

  • Repair any damage.

    If your furniture has any scratches, dents, or other damage, repair it before you list it for sale. You can do this yourself using a repair kit, or you can hire a professional to do it for you.

  • Reupholster your furniture (optional).

    If your furniture is upholstered, you may want to consider reupholstering it. This is a great way to update the look of your furniture and make it more appealing to potential buyers.

  • Add new hardware (optional).

    If your furniture has old or outdated hardware, you can replace it with new hardware. This is a relatively inexpensive way to update the look of your furniture and make it more stylish.

By following these tips, you can clean and repair your office furniture so that it looks its best and is more likely to sell quickly and for a good price.

Take good photos.

Good photos are essential for selling your office furniture online. Potential buyers want to be able to see exactly what they're buying, so make sure your photos are clear, well-lit, and taken from multiple angles.

Here are a few tips for taking good photos of your office furniture:

  • Use a good camera. If you have a DSLR camera, use it to take your photos. If you don't have a DSLR, use the best camera you have available.
  • Take photos in natural light. Natural light is always best for taking photos. If possible, take your photos outdoors or near a window.
  • Take photos from multiple angles. Potential buyers want to be able to see your furniture from all sides. Take photos from the front, the back, the sides, and even the top and bottom.
  • Stage your furniture. Before you take photos of your furniture, stage it so that it looks its best. This means removing any clutter from the background and arranging your furniture in a way that is both attractive and functional.

By following these tips, you can take good photos of your office furniture that will help you sell it quickly and for a good price.

Write a detailed description.

A detailed description is essential for selling your office furniture online. Potential buyers want to know as much as possible about your furniture before they decide to buy it, so be sure to include all the important details in your description.

Here are a few things to include in your description:

  • The type of furniture. What type of furniture is it? Is it a desk, a chair, a filing cabinet, etc.?
  • The dimensions of the furniture. Include the length, width, and height of the furniture.
  • The materials used to make the furniture. What is the furniture made of? Is it wood, metal, plastic, etc.?
  • The condition of the furniture. Is the furniture new, used, or vintage? Are there any scratches, dents, or other damage?
  • Any special features. Does the furniture have any special features, such as a built-in desk or a locking file cabinet?

By including all of these details in your description, you can help potential buyers make an informed decision about whether or not to buy your furniture.

Set a fair price.

Setting a fair price for your office furniture is important for selling it quickly and for getting the most money for it. If you price your furniture too high, potential buyers will be scared away. If you price it too low, you'll end up losing money.

Here are a few things to consider when setting a price for your office furniture:

  • The condition of the furniture. Used furniture will sell for less than new furniture. If your furniture is damaged, you will need to price it accordingly.
  • The type of furniture. Some types of furniture are more in demand than others. For example, desks and chairs tend to sell for more than filing cabinets and bookcases.
  • The brand of the furniture. Well-known brands tend to sell for more than lesser-known brands.
  • The current market value. You can research the current market value of your furniture by looking at similar items for sale online or in stores.

Once you have considered all of these factors, you can set a fair price for your office furniture. It's always better to start with a slightly higher price and then lower it if necessary, than to start with a low price and then try to raise it.

Make the sale safely.

Once you've found a buyer for your office furniture, you need to make sure the sale is completed safely.

  • Meet the buyer in a public place.

    If you're selling your furniture online, meet the buyer in a public place, such as a coffee shop or a police station. This will help protect you in case the buyer is not who they say they are.

  • Get paid before you hand over the furniture.

    Don't hand over the furniture until you have received payment. If the buyer is paying with a check, make sure the check clears before you release the furniture.

  • Get a receipt.

    Once you've received payment, get a receipt from the buyer. This will serve as proof of the sale.

  • Be aware of scams.

    There are a number of scams that target people who are selling furniture online. Be aware of these scams and take steps to protect yourself.

By following these tips, you can make the sale of your office furniture safely and securely.
