Question 2: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?
Answer: When selecting garden furniture, consider the following factors:

  • Material: Choose a material that is durable and weather-resistant.
  • Comfort: Ensure that the furniture is comfortable to sit or lie on.
  • Style: Select furniture that matches your personal taste and the overall design of your garden.
  • Functionality: Consider the intended use of the furniture and choose pieces that suit your needs.
  • Size: Make sure the furniture is proportional to the size of your garden.

Question 3: How can I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: To maintain your garden furniture and extend its lifespan, follow these care tips:

  • Clean regularly: Use mild soap and water to clean your furniture regularly.
  • Protect from harsh weather: Store your furniture indoors or cover it with protective covers during harsh weather conditions.
  • Apply protective treatments: Treat wooden furniture with appropriate oils or sealants to protect it from moisture and UV damage.
  • Tighten screws and bolts: Periodically check and tighten any screws or bolts to ensure stability.
