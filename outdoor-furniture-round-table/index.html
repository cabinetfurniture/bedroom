Picking the Perfect Outdoor Furniture Round Table

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2025 · 12 min read

When it comes to creating a stylish and inviting outdoor space, choosing the right furniture is key. And when it comes to tables, there's no better option than a round table. Round tables are not only visually appealing but also practical, offering ample seating for guests and encouraging conversation. In this article, we'll guide you through the essential factors to consider when selecting the perfect outdoor furniture round table for your garden, patio, or balcony.

Before you start shopping, it's essential to determine the purpose and size of your outdoor space. Consider how many people you typically entertain and how much space you have available. If you have a small space, a smaller round table with a diameter of 36-48 inches would suffice. For larger spaces, you could opt for a table with a diameter of 54-72 inches, providing comfortable seating for up to 8-10 guests.

With the basics covered, you can now explore the various materials, designs, and features available for outdoor furniture round tables.

Round tables are a great choice for outdoor furniture, as they are both stylish and practical.

Durable

When choosing outdoor furniture, durability is key. You want furniture that can withstand the elements and last for many years to come. Round tables are a great option in this regard, as they are typically made from durable materials such as metal, wood, or resin.

  • Metal tables are extremely durable and can withstand all types of weather conditions. They are also easy to clean and maintain.
  • Wood tables are another durable option, but they require more maintenance than metal tables. Wood tables should be treated with a sealant to protect them from the elements.
  • Resin tables are a good choice for those who want a durable and low-maintenance table. Resin tables are made from a synthetic material that is resistant to fading, cracking, and peeling.
  • Wicker tables are also durable, but they are not as weather-resistant as metal, wood, or resin tables. Wicker tables should be covered or stored indoors during inclement weather.

No matter what material you choose, make sure to look for a table that is well-made and has a sturdy construction. This will ensure that your table will last for many years to come.

Versatile

Round tables are not only durable but also versatile. They can be used for a variety of purposes, from dining and entertaining to relaxing and enjoying the outdoors.

  • Dining: Round tables are a great choice for dining, as they provide ample seating for guests and encourage conversation. They are also a good option for small spaces, as they can be pushed up against a wall when not in use.
  • Entertaining: Round tables are also perfect for entertaining, as they provide a comfortable and inviting space for guests to gather. They are also a good choice for parties, as they can accommodate a large number of people.
  • Relaxing: Round tables can also be used for relaxing and enjoying the outdoors. They are a great place to sit and read a book, have a cup of coffee, or simply enjoy the scenery.
  • Multi-purpose: Round tables are also multi-purpose, meaning they can be used for a variety of activities. For example, a round table can be used as a dining table, a coffee table, or even a work table.

No matter how you choose to use it, a round table is a versatile piece of furniture that can add style and functionality to your outdoor space.

Space-saving

Round tables are also a great space-saving option for small outdoor spaces. Unlike rectangular or square tables, round tables do not have any sharp corners, so they can be placed closer together without taking up too much space. This makes them a good choice for balconies, patios, and other small outdoor areas.

Round tables can also be used to create a more intimate and cozy atmosphere. By placing a round table in the center of a small space, you can create a sense of enclosure and privacy. This is a great way to make a small space feel more inviting and comfortable.

In addition, round tables can be used to create a more flexible and dynamic space. Because they do not have any sharp corners, round tables can be easily moved around to accommodate different activities and seating arrangements. This makes them a great choice for spaces that are used for multiple purposes.

Overall, round tables are a great space-saving option for small outdoor spaces. They are also versatile and can be used to create a variety of different atmospheres and seating arrangements. If you are looking for a stylish and functional table for your small outdoor space, a round table is a great option.

Here are some tips for choosing a space-saving round table for your outdoor space:

  • Choose a table with a diameter that is appropriate for the size of your space.
  • Consider a table with a pedestal base, as this will take up less space than a table with four legs.
  • Choose a table with a light color, as this will make the space feel larger.
  • Place the table in the center of the space to create a sense of enclosure and privacy.

Encourages conversation

Round tables are also great for encouraging conversation. Unlike rectangular or square tables, round tables do not have any sharp corners that can create barriers between people. This makes it easier for everyone at the table to see and hear each other, which can lead to more lively and engaging conversations.

  • Equal access: Round tables give everyone at the table equal access to the conversation. There is no head or foot of the table, so everyone can participate equally.
  • No barriers: The lack of sharp corners on a round table creates a more open and inviting atmosphere. This makes it easier for people to make eye contact and engage with each other.
  • More intimate: Round tables create a more intimate and cozy atmosphere than rectangular or square tables. This is because people are closer together and can easily see and hear each other.
  • Encourages participation: The intimate and open atmosphere of a round table encourages everyone to participate in the conversation. This is especially beneficial for shy or introverted people.

If you are looking for a table that will encourage conversation and create a more convivial atmosphere, a round table is a great option.

Aesthetically pleasing

Round tables are also aesthetically pleasing. They have a timeless and elegant design that can complement any outdoor style. Round tables can be made from a variety of materials, including metal, wood, and resin, so you can find a table that matches your taste and décor.

Round tables can also be used to create a variety of different looks. For example, a round table with a metal base and a glass top can create a modern and sophisticated look. A round table with a wooden base and a ceramic top can create a more rustic and homey look. And a round table with a resin base and a wicker top can create a more tropical and relaxed look.

No matter what your style, you can find a round table that will complement your outdoor space and create a beautiful and inviting atmosphere.

Here are some tips for choosing an aesthetically pleasing round table for your outdoor space:

  • Choose a table that is the right size for your space.
  • Choose a table that is made from a durable material that can withstand the elements.
  • Choose a table that has a design that complements your outdoor style.
  • Accessorize your table with cushions, placemats, and centerpieces to create a unique and personal look.

With so many different styles and designs to choose from, you are sure to find a round table that is both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

Available in various materials

Round tables are available in a variety of materials, each with its own unique advantages and disadvantages. Here is a brief overview of the most popular materials used for outdoor furniture round tables:

  • Metal: Metal tables are durable, weather-resistant, and easy to clean. They are also relatively lightweight, making them easy to move around. However, metal tables can be more expensive than other materials.
  • Wood: Wood tables are classic and stylish. They are also durable and can last for many years with proper care. However, wood tables require more maintenance than other materials. They need to be sealed or stained to protect them from the elements, and they should be stored indoors during the winter months.
  • Resin: Resin tables are a good choice for those who want a durable and low-maintenance table. Resin tables are made from a synthetic material that is resistant to fading, cracking, and peeling. They are also lightweight and easy to clean.
  • Wicker: Wicker tables are lightweight and have a natural, beachy look. They are also relatively inexpensive. However, wicker tables are not as durable as other materials and they can fade in the sun. They should also be covered or stored indoors during the winter months.

When choosing a material for your outdoor furniture round table, it is important to consider your needs and budget. If you are looking for a durable and easy-to-care-for table, metal or resin are good options. If you are looking for a classic and stylish table, wood is a good choice. And if you are looking for a lightweight and inexpensive table, wicker is a good option.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and budget. Metal furniture is durable and weather-resistant, but it can be more expensive. Wood furniture is classic and stylish, but it requires more maintenance. Resin furniture is a good choice for those who want a durable and low-maintenance option. Wicker furniture is lightweight and inexpensive, but it is not as durable as other materials.

Question 2: What size garden furniture should I choose?
Answer: The size of garden furniture you choose depends on the size of your garden and the number of people you typically entertain. If you have a small garden, you will want to choose smaller furniture pieces that can be easily moved around. If you have a large garden, you can choose larger furniture pieces that will provide more seating and comfort.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: There are a few things you can do to protect your garden furniture from the elements. First, choose furniture that is made from durable materials that can withstand the sun, rain, and wind. Second, cover your furniture when it is not in use. Third, store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 4: How can I clean my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to clean your garden furniture depends on the material it is made from. For metal furniture, you can use a mild soap and water solution. For wood furniture, you can use a wood cleaner and protector. For resin furniture, you can use a mild soap and water solution or a resin cleaner. For wicker furniture, you can use a soft brush and a mild soap and water solution.

Question 5: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer: There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. First, add cushions and pillows for extra support and comfort. Second, add a throw blanket for warmth on cooler evenings. Third, place your furniture in a shady spot to avoid the sun's glare.

Question 6: How can I style my garden furniture?
Answer: There are many ways to style your garden furniture. You can add plants and flowers to create a more natural look. You can also add throw pillows and blankets to add a pop of color and personality. You can also use outdoor rugs to define the space and add a touch of luxury.
