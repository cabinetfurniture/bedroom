Sustainable and Eco-Friendly

Hadleigh is committed to sustainability and eco-friendliness, and this is reflected in their garden furniture. They use sustainable materials, such as FSC-certified teak and recycled aluminum, and their manufacturing processes are designed to minimize environmental impact.

Here are some of the ways that Hadleigh makes their garden furniture sustainable and eco-friendly:

  • FSC-Certified Teak: Hadleigh uses FSC-certified teak in many of their furniture pieces. FSC certification ensures that the teak is harvested from responsibly managed forests, which helps to protect the environment and promote sustainable forestry practices.
  • Recycled Aluminum: Hadleigh uses recycled aluminum in their aluminum furniture frames. Recycled aluminum has a lower environmental impact than virgin aluminum, as it requires less energy to produce and does not require mining new materials.
  • Powder Coating: Hadleigh uses powder coating on their aluminum furniture frames. Powder coating is a more environmentally friendly alternative to traditional paint, as it does not release harmful VOCs (volatile organic compounds).
  • Water-Based Finishes: Hadleigh uses water-based finishes on their wooden furniture. Water-based finishes are less harmful to the environment than solvent-based finishes, as they do not release harmful VOCs.

By using sustainable materials and eco-friendly manufacturing processes, Hadleigh is helping to reduce their environmental impact and protect the planet for future generations.
