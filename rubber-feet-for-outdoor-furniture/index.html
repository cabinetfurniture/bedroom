One of the biggest benefits of using rubber feet for outdoor furniture is that they can help to prevent rust, rot, and mildew. This is because rubber is a non-porous material, which means that it does not absorb water. When water comes into contact with metal or wood, it can cause the material to rust or rot. However, when water comes into contact with rubber, it simply beads up and rolls off. This makes rubber feet an ideal choice for protecting outdoor furniture from the elements.

Rubber feet can also help to prevent mildew. Mildew is a type of fungus that thrives in damp, humid environments.
