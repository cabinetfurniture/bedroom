Made from recycled materials.

Circular garden furniture is made from a variety of recycled materials, including:

FAQ

If you have any questions about garden furniture, here are some frequently asked questions and their answers:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?

Answer 1: The best material for garden furniture depends on your needs and preferences. Wood is a classic choice that is both durable and stylish. Metal is another popular option, as it is strong and weather-resistant. Plastic is a good choice for budget-minded shoppers, as it is lightweight and easy to clean.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?

Answer 2: When choosing the right size garden furniture, you need to consider the size of your garden and the number of people you will be entertaining. You also need to think about the activities you will be using the furniture for. For example, if you plan on hosting large gatherings, you will need larger furniture than if you are just using it for small family meals.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?

Answer 3: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild detergent and water. You should also protect it from the elements by covering it when it is not in use. If you live in a climate with harsh winters, you may want to store your garden furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 4: How do I make my garden furniture more comfortable?

Answer 4: There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. You can add cushions and pillows for extra padding. You can also add a throw blanket for warmth on cooler evenings. If you have a metal or plastic furniture set, you may want to consider adding a seat cushion for extra comfort.

Question 5: How do I style my garden furniture?

Answer 5: There are many ways to style your garden furniture. You can add colorful cushions and pillows to brighten up the space. You can also add a rug to define the area and make it more inviting. You can also hang string lights or lanterns to create a magical ambiance in the evening.

Question 6: Can I use garden furniture indoors?

Answer 6: Some garden furniture can be used indoors, but not all. Metal and plastic furniture is typically fine to use indoors, but wood furniture may not be suitable for indoor use, as it can be damaged by moisture and humidity.
