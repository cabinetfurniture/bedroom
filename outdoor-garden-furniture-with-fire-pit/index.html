**Safety Features**

When choosing a fire pit, it's important to consider the safety features. Here are some of the most important safety features to look for:

A spark screen is a must-have safety feature for any fire pit. It helps to prevent sparks from flying out of the fire and landing on nearby objects, which could start a fire. A sturdy base is also important for safety. The base should be made of a non-flammable material, such as metal or stone, and it should be wide enough to prevent the fire pit from tipping over. A fire extinguisher should also be kept nearby in case of an emergency. Never leave a fire pit unattended. Always make sure to put out the fire completely before leaving it unattended.

By following these safety tips, you can help to ensure that your fire pit is a safe and enjoyable addition to your outdoor space.
