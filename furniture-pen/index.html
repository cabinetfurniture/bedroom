Easy to use.

One of the biggest advantages of furniture pens is how easy they are to use. You don't need any special skills or tools, and the process is quick and straightforward.

  • Simply shake the pen well before use.

    This will ensure that the ink is evenly mixed and flows smoothly.

  • Apply the pen to the damaged area.

    Hold the pen upright and use light, even strokes to fill in the scratch or nick. Be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this could damage the surface.

  • Let the ink dry completely.

    The drying time will vary depending on the type of furniture pen you are using. Make sure to read the instructions on the pen before moving on to the next step.

  • Buff the area with a soft cloth.

    Once the ink is dry, you can buff the area with a soft cloth to remove any excess ink and create a smooth finish.

That's it! With just a few simple steps, you can easily repair minor imperfections on your furniture using a furniture pen. The process is so easy that anyone can do it, regardless of their skill level.
