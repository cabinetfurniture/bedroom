Measure existing cushions.

Before you start shopping for replacement cushion covers, it's crucial to measure your existing cushions accurately to ensure a perfect fit.

  • Measure the length.

    Place a measuring tape along the longest side of the cushion, from one corner to the opposite corner.

  • Measure the width.

    Place the measuring tape across the widest part of the cushion, perpendicular to the length.

  • Measure the thickness.

    Place the measuring tape vertically, from the top of the cushion to the bottom, at the thickest point.

  • Note the shape.

    Are your cushions square, rectangular, round, or have a unique shape? Make a note of the shape to help you find suitable replacement covers.

Once you have the measurements and shape of your existing cushions, you can start browsing for replacement covers that are the right size and style for your rattan furniture.

Choose weather-resistant fabric.

Since rattan furniture is typically used outdoors, choosing weather-resistant fabric for your replacement cushion covers is essential. This will ensure that your cushions can withstand the elements and maintain their appearance over time.

Here are some factors to consider when selecting weather-resistant fabric:

  • Water resistance: Look for fabrics treated with a water-resistant or waterproof coating to repel moisture and prevent water from seeping into the cushions.
  • UV resistance: Choose fabrics with UV protection to prevent fading and damage caused by prolonged exposure to sunlight.
  • Mold and mildew resistance: Opt for fabrics that are resistant to mold and mildew growth, especially if your patio or deck is prone to humid conditions.
  • Durability: Select fabrics that are durable and can withstand regular use and exposure to the elements without tearing or fading easily.

Some popular weather-resistant fabrics for replacement cushion covers include:

  • Acrylic: Acrylic is a synthetic fabric known for its water resistance, UV resistance, and durability. It is also easy to clean and maintain.
  • Polyester: Polyester is another synthetic fabric that is water-resistant, fade-resistant, and durable. It is also wrinkle-resistant and dries quickly.
  • Canvas: Canvas is a heavy-duty cotton fabric that is water-resistant and durable. It is a good choice for cushions that will be used in high-traffic areas.

By choosing a weather-resistant fabric for your replacement cushion covers, you can ensure that your cushions will last longer and maintain their beauty even when exposed to the elements.
