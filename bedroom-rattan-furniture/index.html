Easy to care for

Rattan furniture is easy to care for, making it a great choice for busy families and those who do not have a lot of time to spend on maintenance.

To clean rattan furniture, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth. You can also use a mild soap solution if necessary. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the rattan.

Rattan furniture should be kept out of direct sunlight and rain, as these can cause the rattan to fade and deteriorate. If your rattan furniture gets wet, dry it off immediately with a clean cloth.

With proper care, rattan furniture can last for many years. By following these simple tips, you can keep your rattan furniture looking its best for years to come.
