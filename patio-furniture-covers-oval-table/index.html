Size: Make sure the cover is large enough to fit your table, but not so large that it's baggy.

The size of your patio furniture cover is important for two reasons. First, you want to make sure that the cover is large enough to fit your table properly. A cover that is too small will not provide adequate protection, and a cover that is too large will be baggy and more likely to blow away in the wind.

To determine the correct size cover for your oval table, measure the length and width of the table. Then, add 2-4 inches to each measurement to allow for some overlap. This will ensure that the cover fits snugly over the table without being too baggy.

In addition to the length and width, you also need to consider the height of your table when choosing a cover. Make sure that the cover is tall enough to cover the entire table, including the legs.

If you are unsure about what size cover to get, it is always better to err on the side of caution and get a cover that is slightly too large rather than too small.

By following these tips, you can choose the right size patio furniture cover for your oval table and keep it looking its best for years to come.

Material: Choose a cover that is made from a durable, weather-resistant material, such as polyester or vinyl.

The material of your patio furniture cover is important because it will determine how well the cover protects your table from the elements. A cover that is made from a durable, weather-resistant material will last longer and provide better protection than a cover that is made from a cheap, flimsy material.

  • Polyester is a synthetic fiber that is known for its durability and resistance to fading. Polyester covers are also water-resistant and mildew-resistant, making them a good choice for outdoor use.
  • Vinyl is another synthetic fiber that is known for its durability and weather resistance. Vinyl covers are also waterproof and mildew-resistant, making them a good choice for areas with harsh weather conditions.
  • Canvas is a natural fiber that is known for its strength and durability. Canvas covers are water-resistant and mildew-resistant, but they are not as durable as polyester or vinyl covers.
  • Plastic is a lightweight and inexpensive material that is often used for patio furniture covers. However, plastic covers are not as durable as polyester or vinyl covers, and they are more likely to tear or fade in the sun.

When choosing a patio furniture cover, it is important to consider the climate in your area and the amount of use that the cover will get. If you live in an area with harsh weather conditions, you will need a cover that is made from a durable, weather-resistant material such as polyester or vinyl. If you live in a mild climate and the cover will only be used occasionally, you may be able to get away with a less expensive cover made from plastic or canvas.

Features: Look for a cover that has features such as a drawstring closure or vents to prevent mildew.

In addition to the size, shape, and material of your patio furniture cover, you should also consider the features that the cover has. Some features to look for include:

Drawstring closure: A drawstring closure helps to keep the cover securely in place, even in windy conditions. This is an important feature if you live in an area with high winds.

Vents: Vents allow air to circulate under the cover, which helps to prevent mildew from forming. Mildew is a fungus that can grow on damp surfaces, and it can cause your patio furniture to deteriorate. Vents are an important feature if you live in a humid climate.

Pockets: Some patio furniture covers have pockets that can be used to store small items, such as cushions or toys. This is a convenient feature if you want to keep your patio area tidy.

Handles: Handles make it easy to put on and take off the cover. This is a helpful feature if you need to access your patio furniture frequently.

By choosing a patio furniture cover with the right features, you can make sure that your table is protected from the elements and that it looks its best for years to come.

Warranty: Choose a cover that comes with a warranty so that you can be sure it will last.

A warranty is a guarantee that the manufacturer will repair or replace the cover if it fails due to a defect in materials or workmanship. A warranty is important because it gives you peace of mind knowing that you are protected if the cover fails.

  • Look for a warranty that covers both the materials and the workmanship of the cover. This will ensure that you are protected if the cover fails due to a defect in either the materials or the construction.
  • Choose a warranty that is long enough to cover the expected lifespan of the cover. Most patio furniture covers come with a warranty of 1-3 years, but some warranties may be longer.
  • Read the warranty carefully before you purchase the cover. Make sure that you understand what is covered by the warranty and what is not.
  • Keep your warranty in a safe place. You will need to provide proof of purchase if you need to make a warranty claim.

By choosing a patio furniture cover with a good warranty, you can be sure that you are protected if the cover fails. This will give you peace of mind knowing that your investment is protected.
