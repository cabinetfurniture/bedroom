Restaurant Furniture Table Tops A Comprehensive Guide

When it comes to designing a restaurant, choosing the right furniture is essential. Not only does it affect the overall aesthetic of the space, but it also impacts the comfort and satisfaction of your customers. One of the most important pieces of restaurant furniture is the table top. The table top is what your customers will be interacting with the most, so it's important to choose one that is durable, attractive, and easy to clean.

There are many different types of restaurant furniture table tops available, each with its own unique advantages and disadvantages. In this guide, we will discuss the different types of table tops and help you choose the best one for your restaurant.

Now that you have a better understanding of the different types of restaurant furniture table tops available, you can start to narrow down your choices. Consider the following factors:

Durability

Durability is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a restaurant furniture table top. Your table tops will be subject to a lot of wear and tear, so it's important to choose a material that is strong and durable. Some of the most durable table top materials include:

  • Laminate
  • Solid wood
  • Metal
  • Stone

Laminate is a type of plastic that is bonded to a substrate material, such as particle board or MDF. It is a very durable and easy-to-clean material, making it a good choice for restaurants with high traffic.

Solid wood is another durable option for restaurant table tops. It is important to choose a hardwood, such as oak or maple, that is resistant to scratches and dents. Solid wood table tops can be refinished if they become damaged, which makes them a good long-term investment.

Metal is a very durable material that is often used for outdoor restaurant table tops. It is important to choose a metal that is resistant to rust and corrosion, such as stainless steel or aluminum.

Stone is the most durable type of table top material. It is very resistant to scratches and heat, and it can be used both indoors and outdoors. However, stone table tops are also the most expensive.

When choosing a restaurant furniture table top, it is important to consider the durability of the material. The table tops will be subject to a lot of wear and tear, so it is important to choose a material that is strong and durable.

Attractiveness

The attractiveness of your restaurant furniture table tops is also an important factor to consider. After all, you want your restaurant to look its best. The table tops should complement the overall design of your restaurant and create a welcoming and inviting atmosphere.

When choosing attractive table tops, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • The shape of the table tops
  • The color of the table tops
  • The material of the table tops

The shape of the table tops can have a big impact on the overall look of your restaurant. Round table tops create a more intimate and cozy atmosphere, while square or rectangular table tops are more formal. You can also choose table tops with unique shapes, such as oval or hexagonal, to add a touch of personality to your restaurant.

The color of the table tops is also an important consideration. You want to choose a color that complements the overall design of your restaurant and creates the desired atmosphere. Light-colored table tops can make a space feel larger and brighter, while dark-colored table tops can create a more intimate and sophisticated atmosphere.

The material of the table tops can also affect their attractiveness. Wood table tops are classic and timeless, while metal table tops are more modern and industrial. You can also choose table tops made from other materials, such as glass or stone, to create a unique look for your restaurant.

By considering the shape, color, and material of your restaurant furniture table tops, you can choose table tops that are both attractive and functional.

Easy to clean

In a restaurant, it is important to keep your furniture clean and sanitary. This is especially true for the table tops, which come into contact with food and drinks on a regular basis. When choosing table tops, it is important to select a material that is easy to clean and disinfect.

Some of the easiest-to-clean table top materials include:

  • Laminate
  • Solid wood
  • Metal
  • Glass

Laminate is a type of plastic that is bonded to a substrate material, such as particle board or MDF. It is a very durable and easy-to-clean material, making it a good choice for restaurants with high traffic.

Solid wood table tops are also relatively easy to clean. However, it is important to use a mild soap and water solution and to avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasives, as these can damage the wood.

Metal table tops are also easy to clean. They can be wiped down with a damp cloth and disinfected with a mild bleach solution.

Glass table tops are very easy to clean. They can be wiped down with a damp cloth and disinfected with a glass cleaner.

When choosing restaurant furniture table tops, it is important to consider how easy they are to clean. This will help you to keep your restaurant clean and sanitary, and it will also save you time and money in the long run.
