Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are weather-resistant and durable. Some popular options include teak, cedar, wrought iron, and resin wicker.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, you can use furniture covers, sealants, and regular cleaning. Store cushions and pillows indoors when not in use.

Question 3: What size garden furniture should I choose?
Answer 3: The size of your garden furniture should be proportionate to the size of your outdoor space. Consider the number of people you typically entertain and the amount of space you have available.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer 4: To make your garden furniture more comfortable, add cushions, pillows, and throws. You can also use an outdoor rug to define the space and add a touch of style.

Question 5: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 5: You should clean your garden furniture regularly, especially during the spring and summer months. Use a mild soap solution and a soft cloth to remove dirt and debris.

Question 6: Can I leave my garden furniture outside during the winter?
Answer 6: It is not advisable to leave your garden furniture outside during the winter in harsh climates. If possible, store your furniture in a dry, sheltered place during the off-season.

Question 7: How can I extend the lifespan of my garden furniture?
Answer 7: To extend the lifespan of your garden furniture, follow these tips: clean it regularly, protect it from the elements, and store it properly during the off-season.
