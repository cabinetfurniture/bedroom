Durable and Long-Lasting

Forbo furniture linoleum is renowned for its exceptional durability, ensuring its beauty and performance for years to come. This remarkable material is highly resistant to wear and tear, making it an ideal choice for high-traffic areas and commercial applications.

Sustainable and Eco-Friendly

Forbo furniture linoleum stands as a champion of sustainability, offering a flooring solution that aligns with the growing global movement towards eco-consciousness. Its production process and composition prioritize environmental conservation, minimizing its ecological footprint.

  • Renewable and Natural Raw Materials: Forbo furniture linoleum is primarily made from linseed oil, a natural and renewable resource. Other raw materials include wood flour, jute, and limestone, which are all sustainably sourced.
  • Low VOC Emissions: Forbo furniture linoleum meets stringent indoor air quality standards, emitting extremely low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). This ensures a healthier indoor environment for occupants.
  • Biodegradable and Recyclable: At the end of its lifecycle, Forbo furniture linoleum can be recycled or biodegraded, minimizing waste and promoting circularity.
  • LEED and BREEAM Compliant: Forbo furniture linoleum contributes to LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) and BREEAM (Building Research Establishment Environmental Assessment Method) certifications, recognized globally for their commitment to sustainable building practices.

By choosing Forbo furniture linoleum, you not only invest in a durable and stylish flooring solution but also make a positive contribution towards environmental sustainability. Hygienic and Easy to Clean

Forbo furniture linoleum is renowned for its hygienic properties and ease of cleaning, making it an ideal choice for spaces that demand high levels of cleanliness and hygiene.

Its naturally bacteriostatic surface inhibits the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms, reducing the risk of infections and promoting a healthier indoor environment. This makes it an excellent choice for healthcare facilities, schools, and other public spaces where hygiene is paramount.

Forbo furniture linoleum is also incredibly easy to clean and maintain. Regular sweeping or vacuuming removes dust and dirt, while occasional damp mopping with a neutral cleaner keeps it looking its best. Its smooth, non-porous surface prevents dirt and spills from penetrating, making it easy to wipe clean without leaving any residue.

Furthermore, Forbo furniture linoleum is resistant to most chemicals and cleaning agents, making it suitable for use with various disinfectants and cleaning solutions. This ensures that it can be thoroughly cleaned and sanitized to meet the highest hygiene standards.

Fire-Resistant and Anti-Static

Forbo furniture linoleum offers an additional layer of safety and functionality with its fire-resistant and anti-static properties.

  • Fire Resistance: Forbo furniture linoleum meets stringent fire safety standards, making it an ideal choice for areas where fire safety is paramount. Its low flame spread and smoke development properties help to minimize the risk of fire and ensure occupant safety.
  • Anti-Static: Forbo furniture linoleum is anti-static, meaning it does not accumulate static electricity. This is particularly beneficial in areas with sensitive electronic equipment or in environments where static discharge can be a concern.

These features make Forbo furniture linoleum a safe and practical flooring solution for various applications, including healthcare facilities, data centers, and areas with high foot traffic.

Comfortable and Warm Underfoot

Forbo furniture linoleum provides a unique combination of comfort and warmth underfoot, making it an ideal choice for spaces where people spend extended periods standing or walking.

This combination of comfort and warmth makes Forbo furniture linoleum an excellent choice for areas such as kitchens, where people often stand for prolonged periods, or in bedrooms, where a warm and inviting atmosphere is desired.
