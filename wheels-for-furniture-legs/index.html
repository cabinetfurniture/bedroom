Easy to install

One of the best things about wheels for furniture legs is that they're easy to install. You don't need any special tools or skills, and you can usually install them in just a few minutes.

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to install wheels on furniture legs:

  1. Remove the old feet from the furniture leg. If the old feet are screwed on, simply unscrew them. If they're nailed on, you can use a hammer and chisel to remove them.
  2. Attach the mounting plate to the furniture leg. The mounting plate is usually included with the wheels. Use screws to attach the mounting plate to the leg, making sure that the screws are tight.
  3. Insert the wheel into the mounting plate. The wheel should fit snugly into the mounting plate. If it's too loose, you can use a washer to shim it up.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for the remaining legs.

Once you've installed the wheels, you can enjoy the newfound mobility of your furniture. You'll be able to easily move it around to clean, rearrange your room, or even take it outside for a change of scenery.
