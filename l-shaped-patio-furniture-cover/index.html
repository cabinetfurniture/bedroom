L Shaped Patio Furniture Covers A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 16, 2025 Â· 12 min read

Patio furniture endures a lot throughout the year. Whether direct sunlight intensifies in the summertime, the harshness of winter's extreme cold sets in, or inclement weather causes unexpected downpours, the elements inevitably take their toll.

Safeguarding your patio furniture is imperative to extend its longevity and maintain its pristine condition. Employing a well-tailored L-shaped patio furniture cover offers an effective solution for guarding your prized outdoor possessions during periods of disuse, especially during prolonged adverse weather conditions.

In the subsequent sections of this article, we delve into the nuances of L-shaped patio furniture covers, guiding you in making informed decisions that align with your specific needs and preferences.

L-Shaped Patio Furniture Cover

Protect your outdoor furniture with a custom-fitted L-shaped patio furniture cover. Here are 8 important points to consider:

  • Durable fabric: withstands wear and tear
  • Waterproof: shields against rain and moisture
  • UV-resistant: prevents fading from sunlight
  • Breathable: allows air circulation to prevent mold
  • Tailored fit: conforms to your furniture's shape
  • Secure fit: adjustable straps and buckles keep it in place
  • Easy to clean: wipe down or hose off
  • Storage bag: convenient for off-season storage

By considering these factors, you can choose the ideal L-shaped patio furniture cover to protect your outdoor investment.

Durable fabric: withstands wear and tear

The fabric of your L-shaped patio furniture cover plays a crucial role in protecting your furniture from the elements and general wear and tear. Here are 4 key points to consider when evaluating the durability of a patio furniture cover:

  • Rip-resistant: The fabric should be strong enough to resist tearing and punctures, especially if you have pets or children who may be rough on the furniture.
Fade-resistant: The fabric should be able to withstand prolonged exposure to sunlight without fading or losing its color. This is especially important if you live in a sunny climate. Waterproof: The fabric should be waterproof to protect your furniture from rain and other moisture. This will help to prevent mold and mildew from forming on your furniture. Breathable: The fabric should be breathable to allow air circulation. This will help to prevent moisture from getting trapped inside the cover and causing mold or mildew to grow.

By choosing a durable fabric for your L-shaped patio furniture cover, you can ensure that your furniture will be protected from the elements and will last for many years to come.

Waterproof: shields against rain and moisture

A waterproof L-shaped patio furniture cover is essential for protecting your furniture from rain and other moisture. This is especially important if you live in a climate with a lot of rain or snow. A waterproof cover will help to keep your furniture dry and prevent it from rotting or rusting.

There are a few things to look for when choosing a waterproof patio furniture cover. First, make sure that the cover is made from a waterproof material, such as vinyl or polyester. Second, check the seams of the cover to make sure that they are sealed and waterproof. Finally, make sure that the cover is large enough to cover your furniture completely.

Once you have chosen a waterproof patio furniture cover, be sure to put it on your furniture whenever it is not in use. This will help to protect your furniture from the elements and extend its lifespan.

Here are a few additional tips for keeping your patio furniture dry:

  • If possible, store your patio furniture in a dry place, such as a garage or shed.
  • If you cannot store your furniture in a dry place, be sure to cover it with a waterproof cover.
  • If your furniture gets wet, be sure to dry it off as soon as possible.

By following these tips, you can help to keep your L-shaped patio furniture dry and protected from the elements.

UV-resistant: prevents fading from sunlight

A UV-resistant L-shaped patio furniture cover is essential for protecting your furniture from the sun's harmful UV rays. UV rays can cause the fabric of your furniture to fade and deteriorate over time. A UV-resistant cover will help to protect your furniture from this damage and keep it looking its best for longer.

There are a few things to look for when choosing a UV-resistant patio furniture cover. First, make sure that the cover is made from a UV-resistant material, such as polyester or canvas. Second, check the UPF rating of the cover. The UPF rating indicates how well the cover protects against UV rays. A UPF rating of 50 or higher is recommended for maximum protection.

Once you have chosen a UV-resistant patio furniture cover, be sure to put it on your furniture whenever it is not in use. This will help to protect your furniture from the sun's harmful UV rays and extend its lifespan.

Here are a few additional tips for protecting your patio furniture from the sun:

  • If possible, place your patio furniture in a shaded area.
  • Use a UV-resistant patio furniture cover whenever your furniture is not in use.
  • Clean your patio furniture regularly to remove dirt and debris that can attract UV rays.

By following these tips, you can help to protect your L-shaped patio furniture from the sun's harmful UV rays and extend its lifespan.

Breathable: allows air circulation to prevent mold

A breathable L-shaped patio furniture cover is important for preventing mold and mildew from forming on your furniture. Mold and mildew thrive in moist, dark environments. A breathable cover will allow air to circulate and help to keep your furniture dry. This will help to prevent mold and mildew from forming and keep your furniture looking its best.

  • Mesh vents: Mesh vents are a great way to allow air to circulate under the cover. Look for a cover with mesh vents on the sides or top.
  • Breathable fabric: The fabric of the cover should be breathable to allow air to pass through. Look for a cover made from a breathable fabric, such as polyester or canvas.
  • Avoid plastic covers: Plastic covers are not breathable and can trap moisture under the cover. This can lead to mold and mildew growth.
  • Regular cleaning: Regularly cleaning your patio furniture will help to remove dirt and debris that can block air flow and promote mold growth.

By choosing a breathable L-shaped patio furniture cover and following these tips, you can help to prevent mold and mildew from forming on your furniture and keep it looking its best for longer.

Secure fit: adjustable straps and buckles keep it in place

A secure fit is essential for keeping your L-shaped patio furniture cover in place, even in high winds. Adjustable straps and buckles are the best way to ensure a secure fit. Here's what to look for:

  • Multiple straps: A cover with multiple straps will provide a more secure fit than a cover with just one or two straps.
  • Adjustable straps: Adjustable straps allow you to customize the fit of the cover to your specific furniture.
  • Buckles: Buckles provide a secure way to fasten the straps and keep the cover in place.

Once you have found a cover with adjustable straps and buckles, be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions for securing the cover to your furniture. This will help to ensure a secure fit and prevent the cover from blowing away in high winds.

Here are a few additional tips for keeping your L-shaped patio furniture cover secure:

  • Tighten the straps regularly: Over time, the straps may loosen. Be sure to tighten them regularly to ensure a secure fit.
  • Use bungee cords: If the cover is still loose, you can use bungee cords to secure it further.
  • Place weights on the cover: If you live in a windy area, you can place weights on the cover to keep it from blowing away.

By following these tips, you can help to ensure that your L-shaped patio furniture cover stays in place, even in the windiest conditions.

Easy to clean: wipe down or hose off

An easy-to-clean L-shaped patio furniture cover will save you time and hassle. Here are a few things to look for when choosing an easy-to-clean cover:

  • Smooth surface: A cover with a smooth surface will be easier to wipe down or hose off than a cover with a rough or textured surface.
  • Fade-resistant: A fade-resistant cover will not lose its color over time, making it easier to keep clean.
  • Machine-washable: If possible, choose a cover that is machine-washable. This will make it easy to clean the cover thoroughly.
  • Zippered openings: Zippered openings make it easy to remove the cover for cleaning.

By choosing an easy-to-clean L-shaped patio furniture cover, you can keep your furniture looking its best with minimal effort.

Storage bag: convenient for off-season storage

A storage bag is a convenient way to store your L-shaped patio furniture cover when it is not in use. Here are a few things to look for when choosing a storage bag:

  • Durable material: The storage bag should be made from a durable material, such as canvas or polyester, to protect the cover from dirt and moisture.
  • Drawstring closure: A drawstring closure will help to keep the cover secure inside the bag.
  • Handles: Handles will make it easy to carry the storage bag.
  • Size: The storage bag should be large enough to fit the cover without being too bulky.

By choosing a durable and convenient storage bag, you can help to extend the life of your L-shaped patio furniture cover and keep it looking its best.
