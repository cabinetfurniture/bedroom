Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are durable, weather-resistant, and easy to maintain. Some popular choices include teak, acacia, wrought iron, and synthetic wicker.

Question 3: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: To protect your garden furniture from sun, rain, and snow, consider using covers or storing it indoors during harsh weather conditions. Regular cleaning and maintenance will also extend its lifespan.

Question 4: Can I use cushions and pillows to enhance the comfort of my garden furniture?
Answer: Yes, cushions and pillows can add comfort and style to your garden furniture. Choose weather-resistant fabrics and store them indoors when not in use to prevent fading and mildew.

Answer: Arrange your garden furniture in a way that encourages conversation and creates a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. Consider using rugs, plants, and other accessories to enhance the ambiance.
