If you're tired of your cat scratching your couch, chairs, or other furniture, there are a few things you can do to stop them.

One of the most effective ways to stop cats from scratching furniture is to use a spray deterrent. There are a number of different sprays on the market that are designed to deter cats from scratching, and they can be a great way to keep your cat from damaging your furniture.

Cats have a strong sense of smell, and they are repelled by certain scents. This is why many spray deterrents contain ingredients that have a strong, unpleasant odor to cats.   • Peppermint: Peppermint is a minty scent that can be very effective at deterring cats. It is also a natural insect repellent, so it can help to keep your furniture free of pests.
  • Lavender: Lavender is a calming scent that is often used in aromatherapy. However, it can also be effective at deterring cats. Some cats find the smell of lavender to be overwhelming.
