Question 1: What materials are your garden furniture made from?
Answer 1: Our garden furniture is crafted from a variety of durable and weather-resistant materials, including teak, acacia, aluminum, and synthetic rattan. These materials are chosen for their longevity and ability to withstand the elements, ensuring that your furniture will look great and last for many years to come.

Question 2: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer 2: Caring for your garden furniture is easy! Regular cleaning with a mild soap solution and water will help keep your furniture looking its best. For wooden furniture, periodic application of a teak protector or oil can help preserve its natural beauty. Synthetic materials, such as rattan and aluminum, require minimal maintenance and can be simply wiped clean.

Question 3: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer 3: Yes, our garden furniture is designed to withstand the elements and can be left outside all year round. However, we recommend storing cushions and other soft furnishings indoors during inclement weather to prolong their lifespan.

Tips

Ready to create your dream outdoor space? Here are four practical tips to help you choose and arrange your garden furniture for maximum style and comfort:

1. Choose the right materials: Consider the climate in your area and the amount of maintenance you're willing to do when selecting materials for your garden furniture. Teak and acacia are durable hardwoods that are naturally weather-resistant, while aluminum and synthetic rattan are low-maintenance options that are easy to clean.

2. Measure your space: Before you start shopping for furniture, measure your outdoor space to determine how much room you have to work with. This will help you choose furniture that is the right size and scale for your area.

3. Create a focal point: A focal point will draw the eye and create a sense of unity in your outdoor space. This could be a statement piece of furniture, such as a large sofa or dining table, or a piece of outdoor art or sculpture.

4. Add cushions and throws: Cushions and throws can add a touch of comfort and style to your garden furniture. Choose fabrics that are weather-resistant and easy to clean, such as Sunbrella or Olefin. And don't be afraid to mix and match colors and patterns to create a unique look.

With these tips in mind, you're well on your way to creating a stylish and comfortable outdoor space that you'll love spending time in.
