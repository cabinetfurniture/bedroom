Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your made to measure garden furniture covers:

Tip 1: Clean your furniture before covering it. This will help to prevent dirt and debris from getting trapped under the cover and damaging your furniture.

Tip 2: Make sure your furniture is completely dry before covering it. If your furniture is wet, it can create a moist environment under the cover that can lead to mold and mildew growth.

Tip 3: Choose the right size cover for your furniture. A cover that is too small will not provide adequate protection, and a cover that is too large can billow in the wind and cause damage to your furniture.

Tip 4: Secure your cover properly. Use the ties or straps that came with your cover to secure it tightly to your furniture. This will help to prevent the cover from blowing off in the wind.

By following these tips, you can help to extend the life of your garden furniture and keep it looking its best for years to come.
