FAQ

Have questions about choosing, caring for, or styling your garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions and create a beautiful outdoor space.

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture?
Answer 1: Consider factors such as the size of your outdoor space, the purpose of the furniture (dining, lounging, etc.), your personal style, and the durability of the materials.

Question 2: What are the best materials for garden furniture?
Answer 2: Common materials include teak, acacia, aluminum, and resin wicker. Teak and acacia are naturally weather-resistant, while aluminum and resin wicker are lightweight and durable.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 3: Use weather-resistant covers when not in use, apply protective oils or sealants to wooden furniture, and store cushions and pillows indoors during inclement weather.

Question 4: How do I clean my garden furniture?
Answer 4: Regular cleaning is essential. Use mild soap, water, and a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that could damage the finish.

Question 5: How can I style my garden furniture?
Answer 5: Add comfortable cushions and throws, incorporate outdoor rugs and lighting, and arrange potted plants and flowers to create a cohesive and inviting atmosphere.
