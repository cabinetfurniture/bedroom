## FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer: The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some popular materials include teak, aluminum, wicker, and plastic. Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. Aluminum is a lightweight and rust-resistant metal that is easy to care for. Wicker is a natural fiber that is woven into furniture frames. It is lightweight and comfortable, but it can be more susceptible to fading and moisture damage. Plastic is a durable and affordable material that is available in a variety of colors and styles.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. You should also consider the size of your furniture in relation to the size of your home. For example, if you have a small patio, you will want to choose furniture that is small and compact. If you have a large backyard, you can choose larger furniture that will provide more seating and comfort.

Question 3: What is the best way to care for garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for garden furniture is to clean it regularly and protect it from the elements. You should clean your furniture with a mild soap and water solution and a soft cloth. You should also apply a sealant or protectant to your furniture to help protect it from the sun, rain, and snow.
