Living Room Wall Furniture A Guide to Enhancing Your Space

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2024 · 19 min read

The living room is the heart of any home, a space where families gather, friends connect, and memories are made. As such, it's essential to create a living room that is both functional and inviting, reflecting your personal style and providing a comfortable ambiance.

Living room wall furniture plays a pivotal role in achieving this balance. From sleek and modern TV stands to cozy and functional bookcases, the right wall furniture can transform your living room into a cohesive and stylish space. In this article, we will delve into the different types of living room wall furniture, providing tips and recommendations to help you find the perfect pieces for your home.

Before we explore the various types of living room wall furniture, it's important to consider your room's layout, style, and functionality. By understanding the specific needs of your living space, you can make informed decisions that will enhance its overall aesthetic and comfort.

Size: Ensure the furniture is appropriately sized for your room.

The size of your living room wall unit should be appropriate for the size of your room. A unit that is too large will overwhelm the space, while a unit that is too small will look lost. To choose the right size unit, measure the wall where you plan to place it and then consider the following factors:

  • The height of the unit. The unit should be tall enough to provide adequate storage and display space, but not so tall that it overwhelms the room.
  • The width of the unit. The unit should be wide enough to accommodate your storage and display needs, but not so wide that it blocks the flow of traffic in the room.
  • The depth of the unit. The unit should be deep enough to provide adequate storage space, but not so deep that it juts out into the room and becomes a hazard.
  • The scale of the unit. The unit should be in proportion to the size of the room. A large unit in a small room will look out of place, while a small unit in a large room will look lost.

By considering these factors, you can choose a living room wall unit that is the right size for your space and that creates a balanced and harmonious look.

Lighting: Consider how lighting will affect the overall look and functionality of your furniture.

The lighting in your living room can have a significant impact on the overall look and functionality of your wall unit. Natural light can make your unit look brighter and more inviting, while artificial light can create a more dramatic or intimate atmosphere. Here are some things to consider when choosing lighting for your living room wall unit:

  • Consider the natural light in your room. If your living room has a lot of natural light, you may not need to add much artificial light. However, if your living room is dark or has limited natural light, you will need to add more artificial light to make your wall unit visible and functional.
  • Consider the overall style of your living room. If you have a modern living room, you may want to choose lighting that is clean and simple. If you have a more traditional living room, you may want to choose lighting that is more ornate.
  • Consider the function of your wall unit. If you use your wall unit for storage, you will need to choose lighting that is bright enough to see what you are looking for. If you use your wall unit for display, you may want to choose lighting that is more focused on highlighting the items on display.

Here are some specific types of lighting that you can use to enhance your living room wall unit:

  • Recessed lighting. Recessed lighting is a great way to add general lighting to your living room without taking up any floor space. Recessed lights can be installed in the ceiling or walls, and they can be directed to shine on your wall unit.
  • Pendant lighting. Pendant lights are a great way to add a touch of style to your living room while also providing light. Pendant lights can be hung from the ceiling, and they can be used to create a focal point over your wall unit.
  • Wall sconces. Wall sconces are a great way to add ambient light to your living room. Wall sconces can be mounted on the wall, and they can be used to highlight your wall unit or create a cozy atmosphere.
  • LED strip lighting. LED strip lighting is a great way to add a touch of color and excitement to your living room. LED strip lighting can be mounted under or behind your wall unit, and it can be used to create a variety of different effects.

By carefully considering the lighting in your living room, you can create a space that is both stylish and functional. Lighting can be used to highlight your wall unit, create a cozy atmosphere, and make it easier to see what you are looking for.
