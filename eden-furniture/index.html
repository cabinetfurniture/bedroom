Sustainable materials

Our wood is sourced from sustainably managed forests, and our fabrics are made from recycled materials whenever possible. We also use low-VOC (volatile organic compound) finishes to protect our furniture and the environment.

Here are some of the sustainable materials we use in our furniture:

  • FSC-certified wood: The Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) is a non-profit organization that promotes responsible forest management. FSC-certified wood comes from forests that are managed in a sustainable way, ensuring that they remain healthy and productive for future generations.
  • Recycled fabrics: We use recycled fabrics in many of our upholstered pieces. Recycled fabrics are made from post-consumer waste, such as plastic bottles and old clothes. By using recycled fabrics, we help to reduce the amount of waste going to landfills and conserve valuable resources.
  • Low-VOC finishes: VOCs are chemicals that can be harmful to human health and the environment. We use low-VOC finishes to protect our furniture and the environment. Low-VOC finishes emit fewer VOCs, which helps to improve indoor air quality.

Eden Furniture is committed to sustainability and using eco-friendly materials in all of our products. We believe that it is important to protect the planet for future generations.

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer: The best materials for garden furniture are those that are durable and weather-resistant. Some of the most popular materials include teak, aluminum, and wicker.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer: When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you will be entertaining. You should also think about the type of activities you will be using the furniture for.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly and protect it from the elements. You should clean your furniture with a mild soap and water solution and dry it thoroughly. You should also apply a sealant to protect it from the sun and rain.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer: You can make your garden furniture more comfortable by adding cushions and pillows. You can also add a throw blanket for extra warmth on cooler evenings.

Question 5: How do I store my garden furniture during the winter?
Answer: If you live in a climate with cold winters, you will need to store your garden furniture during the winter months. You can store your furniture in a garage, shed, or basement. You should also cover your furniture with a tarp or furniture cover to protect it from the elements.

Question 6: How do I choose the right style of garden furniture?
Answer: The best way to choose the right style of garden furniture is to consider the overall style of your home and garden. You should also think about the type of activities you will be using the furniture for.
