Easy to clean

One of the best things about cushion covers for rattan garden furniture is that they are very easy to clean.

  • Machine washable:

    Many cushion covers are machine washable, which makes them very easy to clean. Simply remove the covers from the cushions and wash them in the washing machine according to the care instructions on the label.

  • Spot clean:

    If the cushion covers are not machine washable, you can spot clean them with a mild detergent and a damp cloth. Simply apply the detergent to the stain and rub gently. Rinse the area with water and allow the covers to air dry.

  • Dry quickly:

    Cushion covers for rattan garden furniture are made from materials that dry quickly. This means that you can wash them and have them dry in no time, so you can enjoy your garden furniture again as soon as possible.

  • Fade resistant:

    Many cushion covers are fade resistant, which means that they will not fade in the sun. This is important for outdoor furniture, as the cushions will be exposed to the sun and other elements.

By following these simple care instructions, you can keep your cushion covers for rattan garden furniture looking their best for years to come.
