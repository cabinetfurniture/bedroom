Durability: Choose furniture made from weather-resistant materials like teak or aluminum.

When choosing garden furniture in Glasgow, durability is key. The unpredictable Scottish weather can take its toll on outdoor furniture, so it's important to select pieces that can withstand the elements and provide years of enjoyment.

Teak is a naturally weather-resistant hardwood that is perfect for outdoor use. It is strong, durable, and resistant to rot and decay. Teak furniture will gradually develop a silvery-gray patina over time, which adds to its charm and character.

Aluminum is another excellent choice for weather-resistant garden furniture. It is lightweight, rust-proof, and easy to maintain. Aluminum furniture is also available in a variety of styles and colors, so you can find pieces that complement your outdoor dÃ©cor.
