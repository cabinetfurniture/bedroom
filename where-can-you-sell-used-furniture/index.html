Where Can You Sell Used Furniture

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2025 · 10 min read

Are you decluttering to make room for new furniture or need to get rid of some old pieces? Selling used furniture is a great way to make some extra cash and give your old furniture a new life.

There are many different places where you can sell used furniture, both online and offline. The best option for you will depend on factors such as the type of furniture you have, how much time you have to sell it, and how much money you want to make.

Where Can You Sell Used Furniture?

Here are five important points to consider when selling used furniture:

  • Online marketplaces: Websites like eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Craigslist are great places to sell used furniture online.
  • Consignment stores: Consignment stores sell used furniture on your behalf for a commission.
  • Thrift stores: Thrift stores sell used furniture at a discounted price, and they often donate the proceeds to charity.
  • Garage sales: Garage sales are a great way to sell used furniture locally.
  • Local classified ads: Local classified ads in newspapers or online can be a good way to sell used furniture to people in your area.

The best place to sell used furniture will depend on factors such as the type of furniture you have, how much time you have to sell it, and how much money you want to make.

Online marketplaces: Websites like eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Craigslist are great places to sell used furniture online.

Online marketplaces are a great option for selling used furniture because they reach a large audience of potential buyers. eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Craigslist are three of the most popular online marketplaces for selling used furniture.

eBay is an auction-style marketplace where you can list your furniture for sale and buyers can bid on it. This can be a good option if you have a unique or valuable piece of furniture that you think will attract a lot of interest. However, eBay does charge listing fees and final value fees, so be sure to factor those costs into your decision.

Facebook Marketplace is a free online marketplace where you can list your furniture for sale and buyers can contact you directly. This is a good option if you want to sell your furniture locally and avoid shipping costs. However, Facebook Marketplace can be more competitive than other online marketplaces, so it's important to price your furniture competitively and take good photos.

Craigslist is a classifieds website where you can list your furniture for sale for free. This is a good option if you want to sell your furniture quickly and easily. However, Craigslist can be more anonymous than other online marketplaces, so it's important to be careful when meeting potential buyers.

When selling used furniture online, it's important to take good photos, write a detailed description, and price your furniture competitively. You should also be prepared to answer questions from potential buyers and be willing to negotiate on price.

Consignment stores: Consignment stores sell used furniture on your behalf for a commission.

Consignment stores are a great option for selling used furniture if you don't have the time or inclination to sell it yourself. Consignment stores sell used furniture on your behalf for a commission, typically between 20% and 50%. This means that you don't have to deal with the hassle of listing your furniture, taking photos, or negotiating with potential buyers.

  • Pros of selling used furniture through a consignment store:

    - You don't have to deal with the hassle of listing your furniture, taking photos, or negotiating with potential buyers.
    - Consignment stores typically have a good reputation and can help you get a fair price for your furniture.
    - Consignment stores can also help you stage your furniture and make it look its best.

  • Cons of selling used furniture through a consignment store:

    - You will have to pay a commission to the consignment store, typically between 20% and 50%.
    - It may take some time for your furniture to sell, depending on the store's inventory and the demand for your type of furniture.
    - You may not get as much money for your furniture as you would if you sold it yourself.

Overall, consignment stores are a good option for selling used furniture if you don't have the time or inclination to sell it yourself. However, you should be aware of the commission fees and the fact that it may take some time for your furniture to sell.

Thrift stores: Thrift stores sell used furniture at a discounted price, and they often donate the proceeds to charity.

Thrift stores are a great option for selling used furniture if you want to get rid of it quickly and easily. Thrift stores typically sell used furniture at a discounted price, and they often donate the proceeds to charity. This means that you can feel good about donating your used furniture to a good cause.

There are a few things to keep in mind when selling used furniture to a thrift store. First, thrift stores typically only accept furniture that is in good condition. This means that your furniture should be free of any major damage or stains. Second, thrift stores typically have limited space, so they may not be able to accept all of your furniture. It's a good idea to call ahead to the thrift store to see if they are accepting furniture donations.

If you have a lot of used furniture to sell, you may want to consider having a garage sale. Garage sales are a great way to sell used furniture quickly and easily. However, you will need to be prepared to price your furniture competitively and to deal with the hassle of having people come to your home to buy it.

Overall, thrift stores are a great option for selling used furniture if you want to get rid of it quickly and easily and support a good cause. However, it's important to keep in mind that thrift stores typically only accept furniture that is in good condition and that they may not be able to accept all of your furniture.

Local classified ads: Local classified ads in newspapers or online can be a good way to sell used furniture to people in your area.

Local classified ads are a good option for selling used furniture if you want to sell it locally and avoid shipping costs. You can place classified ads in local newspapers or online classifieds websites. When placing a classified ad, be sure to include a clear description of your furniture, including the condition, dimensions, and price. You should also include good quality photos of your furniture.

One advantage of selling used furniture through local classified ads is that you can meet potential buyers in person to inspect the furniture before they buy it. This can help to reduce the risk of fraud or scams. However, it's important to be careful when meeting potential buyers in person. Always meet in a public place and be aware of your surroundings.

Another advantage of selling used furniture through local classified ads is that you can often get a higher price than you would if you sold it through a consignment store or thrift store. However, it's important to price your furniture competitively and be willing to negotiate on price.

Overall, local classified ads are a good option for selling used furniture if you want to sell it locally and avoid shipping costs. However, it's important to be aware of the potential risks and to take precautions to protect yourself from fraud or scams.
