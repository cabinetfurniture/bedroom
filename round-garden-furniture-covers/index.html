Durable materials

The durability of your round garden furniture cover is paramount to its effectiveness in protecting your furniture. Covers made from high-quality materials will withstand the elements and last for several seasons, providing you with peace of mind and value for your investment.

  • Rip-resistant fabrics: Look for covers made from durable fabrics such as polyester or canvas. These fabrics are resistant to tearing and puncturing, ensuring that your cover can withstand unexpected snags or sharp objects.
  • UV-resistant materials: Prolonged exposure to the sun's UV rays can cause fabrics to fade and deteriorate. Choose covers made with UV-resistant materials to prevent fading and extend the lifespan of your cover.
  • Waterproof coatings: A waterproof coating is essential for protecting your furniture from rain and moisture. Look for covers with a waterproof rating of at least 600mm to ensure adequate protection.
  • Reinforced seams: The seams of your cover are potential weak points where tearing can occur. Choose covers with reinforced or double-stitched seams for added durability.

By opting for a round garden furniture cover made from durable materials, you can trust that your outdoor furniture will remain protected from the elements, season after season.

Waterproof and UV-resistant

Protecting your round garden furniture from rain and sun damage is crucial for maintaining its condition and extending its lifespan. Waterproof and UV-resistant round garden furniture covers provide a reliable barrier against these elements.

  • Waterproof fabrics: Waterproof fabrics, such as polyester with a polyurethane coating, effectively repel water and prevent moisture from penetrating the cover. This protection ensures that your furniture stays dry and protected, even during heavy rainfall.
  • Taped seams: Taped seams add an extra layer of protection by sealing the stitching holes where water could potentially seep through. Look for covers with taped seams to ensure complete waterproofness.

By choosing a round garden furniture cover that is both waterproof and UV-resistant, you can safeguard your furniture from the damaging effects of rain and sun exposure, ensuring its longevity and beauty for seasons to come.

Proper fit

Ensuring a proper fit for your round garden furniture cover is essential to guarantee effective protection. A cover that is too loose can flap in the wind and potentially damage your furniture, while a cover that is too tight can put unnecessary stress on the seams and fabric.

To achieve a proper fit, measure the diameter of your round furniture piece and choose a cover that is specifically designed for that size. Most covers will have a sizing chart or guide to help you make the right selection.

In addition to the diameter, also consider the height of your furniture. Some covers are designed to fit snugly over the entire piece, while others may have a more relaxed fit. Choose a cover that provides adequate coverage without being excessively baggy.

If you have any doubts about the fit, it's always better to opt for a slightly larger cover. You can cinch it at the bottom using the drawstring or toggle closure to achieve a secure fit.

A properly fitted round garden furniture cover will not only protect your furniture from the elements but also enhance its appearance by giving it a neat and tidy look.

Drawstring or toggle closure

A drawstring or toggle closure is a common feature on round garden furniture covers, providing a secure and adjustable fit. Here's how it works:

**Drawstring closure:** A drawstring closure consists of a cord or rope that runs through a channel around the bottom edge of the cover. To secure the cover, simply pull the drawstring tight and tie it into a knot. This type of closure allows you to customize the fit and ensure a snug fit.

**Toggle closure:** A toggle closure uses a cord or rope that is threaded through a series of toggles or loops sewn into the bottom edge of the cover. To secure the cover, pull the cord tight and insert the toggles into the corresponding loops. This type of closure is quick and easy to use, providing a secure fit.

Both drawstring and toggle closures allow you to adjust the tightness of the cover, accommodating furniture pieces of slightly different sizes. They also make it easy to remove the cover when needed.

Air vents

Air vents are an essential feature of round garden furniture covers, as they allow for proper ventilation and prevent moisture build-up. Here's why air vents are important:

**Prevent condensation:** When warm, moist air inside the cover comes into contact with the cooler cover material, condensation can form. This moisture can lead to mold and mildew growth, which can damage your furniture and the cover itself.

**Reduce wind resistance:** Air vents allow wind to pass through the cover, reducing wind resistance and preventing the cover from billowing and potentially damaging your furniture or the cover itself.

**Maintain airflow:** Proper airflow helps to prevent the cover from sticking to your furniture, making it easier to remove when needed.

When choosing a round garden furniture cover with air vents, ensure that the vents are positioned strategically to allow for maximum airflow. Look for covers with multiple vents to ensure adequate ventilation.

Easy to clean

Keeping your round garden furniture cover clean is essential to maintain its functionality and appearance. Here are some points to consider when choosing a cover that is easy to clean:

  • Machine-washable fabrics: Choose covers made from machine-washable fabrics, such as polyester or nylon, for easy cleaning. Machine washing allows you to thoroughly remove dirt, debris, and stains.
  • Smooth surfaces: Covers with smooth surfaces are less likely to trap dirt and debris, making them easier to wipe clean with a damp cloth.
  • Fade-resistant materials: Opt for covers made from fade-resistant materials to prevent colors from running or fading during washing.
  • Quick-drying fabrics: Choose covers made from quick-drying fabrics to minimize the risk of mold and mildew growth, especially after washing.

By choosing a round garden furniture cover that is easy to clean, you can maintain its appearance and extend its lifespan, ensuring that your outdoor furniture remains protected for seasons to come.

Storage bag included

A storage bag is a convenient accessory that can extend the lifespan of your round garden furniture cover and keep it organized when not in use. Here are some points to consider when choosing a cover that comes with a storage bag:

  • Durable materials: Choose a storage bag made from durable materials, such as polyester or canvas, to withstand outdoor conditions and protect the cover from damage.
  • Drawstring or zipper closure: Opt for a storage bag with a drawstring or zipper closure to keep the cover securely contained and prevent dust or dirt from entering.
  • Adequate size: Ensure that the storage bag is large enough to accommodate the cover without being too bulky or difficult to store.
  • Convenient handles: Choose a storage bag with convenient handles for easy carrying and handling.

By choosing a round garden furniture cover that comes with a storage bag, you can keep your cover protected, organized, and easily accessible when needed.

Warranty

A warranty provides peace of mind and protects your investment in a round garden furniture cover. Here are some points to consider when choosing a cover with a warranty:
