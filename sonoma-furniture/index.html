Sustainable materials

At Sonoma Furniture, we are committed to sustainability. We believe that it is our responsibility to protect the environment for future generations. That's why we use sustainable materials in all of our furniture.

  • FSC-certified wood

    All of the wood we use in our furniture is FSC-certified. This means that it comes from forests that are managed responsibly, ensuring that they remain healthy and productive for future generations.

  • Recycled materials

    We use recycled materials whenever possible, such as recycled metal and plastic. This helps to reduce our environmental impact and conserve natural resources.

  • Low-VOC finishes

    We use low-VOC (volatile organic compound) finishes on all of our furniture. VOCs are harmful chemicals that can pollute the air and contribute to climate change. By using low-VOC finishes, we help to create a healthier indoor environment.

  • Sustainable packaging

    We use sustainable packaging materials, such as recycled cardboard and paper. This helps to reduce waste and protect the environment.

By using sustainable materials, we are helping to protect the environment and create a more sustainable future. We believe that it is important to make choices that are good for both our customers and the planet.

FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've got you covered. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about garden furniture, along with their answers:

Question 1: What are the most durable materials for garden furniture?
Answer: The most durable materials for garden furniture are teak, aluminum, and wrought iron. These materials are weather-resistant and can withstand the elements without rusting or rotting.

Question 2: What are the most comfortable materials for garden furniture?
Answer: The most comfortable materials for garden furniture are wicker, textilene, and cushioned fabrics. These materials are soft and supportive, making them ideal for relaxing in your garden.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: You can protect your garden furniture from the elements by using a furniture cover, storing it in a garage or shed, or treating it with a weather-resistant sealant.

Question 4: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer: You should clean your garden furniture regularly to remove dirt and debris. The frequency of cleaning will depend on the material of your furniture and the weather conditions in your area.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer: It is not recommended to leave your garden furniture outside all year round, as the elements can damage it over time. If you must leave your furniture outside, be sure to protect it from the elements with a furniture cover or weather-resistant sealant.

Question 6: Where can I buy quality garden furniture?
Answer: You can buy quality garden furniture from a variety of retailers, including online retailers, home improvement stores, and furniture stores. Be sure to do your research to find a reputable retailer that sells high-quality furniture at a fair price.

Tips

Now that you have your garden furniture, here are a few tips to help you choose, care for, and enjoy it for years to come:

Tip 1: Choose the right materials. The best materials for garden furniture are teak, aluminum, and wrought iron. These materials are weather-resistant and can withstand the elements without rusting or rotting.

Tip 2: Consider comfort. If you're going to be spending a lot of time in your garden furniture, make sure it's comfortable. Look for furniture with soft, supportive cushioning and adjustable features.

Tip 3: Protect your furniture from the elements. When you're not using your garden furniture, cover it with a furniture cover or store it in a garage or shed. This will help to protect it from the sun, rain, and other elements.

Tip 4: Clean your furniture regularly. To keep your garden furniture looking its best, clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. Be sure to rinse the furniture thoroughly after cleaning.

By following these tips, you can keep your garden furniture looking beautiful and lasting for years to come.
