Easy to apply and maintain

Furniture wax clear is incredibly easy to apply and maintain, making it a convenient choice for furniture care. Here's a step-by-step guide to applying furniture wax clear:

  • Clean the furniture: Before applying wax, make sure the furniture is clean and free of dust and dirt. Use a soft cloth to wipe away any debris.
  • Apply a thin coat of wax: Using a clean cloth or applicator pad, apply a thin, even coat of furniture wax clear to the surface of the furniture. Work in small sections, applying the wax in the direction of the grain.
  • Buff the wax: Once you've applied the wax, use a clean cloth to buff it into the wood. This will help to distribute the wax evenly and remove any excess.
  • Allow the wax to dry: Allow the wax to dry completely according to the manufacturer's instructions. This may take several hours or overnight.

To maintain the wax finish, simply dust the furniture regularly with a soft cloth. You can also reapply wax every few months or as needed to keep your furniture looking its best.
