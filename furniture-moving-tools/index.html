Hassle Free Furniture Moving Tools for a Stress Free Move

Written by Robby Apr 07, 2024 Â· 11 min read

Moving furniture can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have the right tools. Whether you're moving into a new home or simply rearranging your existing space, having the necessary equipment can make all the difference. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide to furniture moving tools, helping you choose the essential items for a stress-free furniture moving experience.

Before you embark on your furniture moving journey, it's important to assess the scope of your task. Consider the size and quantity of furniture you need to move, as well as the distance and accessibility of your new or rearranged space. This will help you determine the specific tools and equipment you require.

Now that you have a clear understanding of your moving needs, let's delve into the world of furniture moving tools and explore the options available to you.

furniture moving tools

Furniture moving tools are essential for making your move as smooth and efficient as possible. Here are seven important points to consider when choosing the right tools for the job:

  • Furniture dolly: A must-have for moving heavy furniture.
  • Furniture sliders: Protect your floors and make furniture easier to move.
  • Lifting straps: Provide extra support and reduce strain.
  • Furniture blankets: Protect your furniture from scratches and damage.
  • Screwdriver or drill: For disassembling and reassembling furniture.
  • Wrench: For tightening and loosening bolts.
  • Level: Ensure your furniture is level and stable.

By having the right tools on hand, you can make your furniture moving experience less stressful and more efficient.

Furniture dolly: A must-have for moving heavy furniture.

A furniture dolly is an essential tool for moving heavy furniture. It is a platform with wheels that allows you to move furniture easily and safely. Furniture dollies are available in a variety of sizes and capacities, so you can choose one that is appropriate for the size and weight of your furniture.

  • Stability: Furniture dollies provide a stable base for moving furniture, reducing the risk of it tipping over or falling.
  • Effort reduction: Dollies make it easier to move heavy furniture by distributing the weight over the wheels.
  • Floor protection: The wheels of a furniture dolly help protect your floors from scratches and damage.
  • Convenience: Furniture dollies are easy to use and can be maneuvered through doorways and hallways with ease.

Overall, a furniture dolly is an invaluable tool for moving heavy furniture safely and efficiently. It can save you time, effort, and potential damage to your furniture and floors.

Furniture sliders: Protect your floors and make furniture easier to move.

Furniture sliders are an excellent way to protect your floors and make moving furniture easier. They are thin, disc-shaped pads that are placed under the legs of furniture to allow it to slide easily across the floor. Furniture sliders are made from a variety of materials, including felt, plastic, and rubber, and are available in a range of sizes to accommodate different furniture types.

Furniture sliders work by reducing the friction between the furniture and the floor. This makes it much easier to move furniture, even heavy pieces, without having to lift or carry them. Furniture sliders also help to protect your floors from scratches, dents, and other damage that can occur when moving furniture.

Furniture sliders are easy to use. Simply place them under the legs of the furniture you want to move, and then slide the furniture into place. Furniture sliders can be used on a variety of floor types, including hardwood, tile, laminate, and carpet.

Overall, furniture sliders are a valuable tool for anyone who needs to move furniture. They are inexpensive, easy to use, and can help to protect your floors and make moving furniture easier.

Here are some additional benefits of using furniture sliders:

  • They can help to reduce noise when moving furniture.
  • They can make it easier to rearrange furniture.
  • They can be used to move furniture over thresholds and other obstacles.

Lifting straps: Provide extra support and reduce strain.

Lifting straps are an essential tool for anyone who needs to move heavy furniture. They are designed to provide extra support and reduce strain on your back, arms, and shoulders.

  • Ergonomic design: Lifting straps are designed to fit comfortably around your body, distributing the weight of the furniture evenly.
  • Adjustable: Lifting straps are adjustable, so you can customize them to fit your body size and the weight of the furniture you are moving.
  • Durable: Lifting straps are made from durable materials, such as nylon and polyester, so they can withstand the weight of heavy furniture.
  • Easy to use: Lifting straps are easy to use. Simply wrap them around the furniture and lift.

Overall, lifting straps are a valuable tool for anyone who needs to move heavy furniture. They can help to reduce strain and prevent injuries.

Furniture blankets: Protect your furniture from scratches and damage.

Furniture blankets are an essential tool for protecting your furniture from scratches and damage during a move. They are made from thick, durable material, such as canvas or quilted cotton, and are available in a variety of sizes to accommodate different furniture items.

Furniture blankets work by creating a barrier between the furniture and the moving equipment. This helps to prevent scratches, dings, and other damage that can occur during transit. Furniture blankets can also be used to protect furniture from dust and dirt during storage.

To use furniture blankets, simply wrap them around the furniture item you want to protect. Secure the blankets with tape or twine, and then load the furniture onto the moving truck or trailer.

Furniture blankets are a relatively inexpensive way to protect your furniture during a move. They are also easy to use and can be reused multiple times.

Here are some additional benefits of using furniture blankets:

  • They can help to keep your furniture organized during a move.
  • They can be used to protect furniture from the elements, such as sun and rain.
  • They can be used as padding for fragile items, such as artwork.

Screwdriver or drill: For disassembling and reassembling furniture.

A screwdriver or drill is an essential tool for disassembling and reassembling furniture. It is used to remove and tighten screws, which are the most common type of fastener used in furniture construction.

  • Variety of sizes: Screwdrivers and drills come in a variety of sizes and shapes to accommodate different types of screws.
  • Manual or electric: Screwdrivers can be manual or electric. Manual screwdrivers are less expensive, but electric screwdrivers are faster and easier to use.
  • Magnetic tips: Many screwdrivers and drills have magnetic tips, which help to hold screws in place.
  • Drill bits: Drills can be used to drill holes in wood, metal, and other materials. This can be helpful for assembling furniture that requires bolts or other fasteners.

Overall, a screwdriver or drill is a versatile tool that is essential for anyone who needs to disassemble or reassemble furniture.

Wrench: For tightening and loosening bolts.

A wrench is a tool used to tighten and loosen bolts. It is a simple tool, but it is essential for assembling and disassembling furniture.

Wrenches come in a variety of sizes and shapes to accommodate different types of bolts. The most common type of wrench is the adjustable wrench, which can be used to tighten and loosen bolts of different sizes.

To use a wrench, simply place the jaws of the wrench over the bolt and turn. The wrench will grip the bolt and allow you to tighten or loosen it.

Here are some additional tips for using a wrench:

  • Always use the correct size wrench for the bolt you are working on.
  • Make sure the wrench is properly seated on the bolt before you turn it.
  • Apply even pressure to the wrench when you are turning it.
  • Do not overtighten bolts.

A wrench is a versatile tool that can be used for a variety of tasks. It is an essential tool for anyone who needs to assemble or disassemble furniture.

Level: Ensure your furniture is level and stable.

A level is a tool used to ensure that furniture is level and stable. It is a simple tool, but it is essential for making sure that your furniture is safe and comfortable to use.

  • Accuracy: Levels are designed to be accurate, so you can be sure that your furniture is level.
  • Easy to use: Levels are easy to use. Simply place the level on the surface of the furniture and read the bubble.
  • Compact: Levels are compact and easy to store, so you can keep them on hand for whenever you need them.
  • Affordable: Levels are affordable, so you can get one without breaking the bank.

Overall, a level is an essential tool for anyone who wants to make sure that their furniture is level and stable.

Tips

Here are some tips for choosing and caring for your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right material
The best material for garden furniture depends on your individual needs and preferences. Some popular materials include teak, acacia, wrought iron, and plastic. Teak and acacia are weather-resistant and durable, making them a good choice for outdoor furniture. Wrought iron is also durable, but it can rust if it is not properly cared for. Plastic is a lightweight and affordable option, but it may not be as durable as other materials.

Tip 2: Consider your space
When choosing garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your space. Measure your space before you start shopping, and then choose furniture that is appropriately sized for your space. You don't want to overcrowd your space with too much furniture, but you also want to make sure that you have enough furniture to accommodate your needs.

Tip 3: Choose comfortable furniture
You're going to be spending a lot of time relaxing in your garden furniture, so it's important to choose furniture that is comfortable. Look for furniture with cushions and pillows that provide support and comfort.

Tip 4: Protect your furniture
To extend the life of your garden furniture, it is important to protect it from the elements. Clean your furniture regularly with a mild soap and water solution, and then rinse it thoroughly. To protect your furniture from the elements, cover it with a tarp or store it in a garage or shed during the winter months.
