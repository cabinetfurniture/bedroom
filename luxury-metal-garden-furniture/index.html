FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture to help you make an informed decision:

Question 1: What are the most durable materials for garden furniture?
Answer: The most durable materials for garden furniture are wrought iron, cast aluminum, and stainless steel. These metals are resistant to rust, corrosion, and extreme weather conditions.

Question 2: How do I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer: To protect your garden furniture from the elements, you can use protective covers, sealants, or rust-resistant finishes.

Question 3: How often should I clean my garden furniture?
Answer: You should clean your garden furniture regularly, especially during the spring and fall. Use mild soap and water, and avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners.

Question 4: Can I leave my garden furniture outdoors all year round?
Answer: Whether or not you can leave your garden furniture outdoors all year round depends on the climate you live in and the materials your furniture is made from. If you live in a harsh climate, you may need to store your furniture indoors during the winter months.

Question 5: How do I choose the right size garden furniture for my space?
Answer: To choose the right size garden furniture for your space, you need to consider the dimensions of your outdoor area and the number of people you want to accommodate.

Question 6: What are the latest trends in garden furniture?
