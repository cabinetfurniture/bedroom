Supports circular economy

When you buy second hand furniture, you're not only saving money and reducing waste, you're also supporting a circular economy. A circular economy is a model of production and consumption that aims to eliminate waste and pollution by keeping resources in use for as long as possible.

In a linear economy, we take raw materials, make products, use them, and then throw them away. This model is unsustainable because it leads to a build-up of waste and pollution. In contrast, a circular economy keeps resources in use for as long as possible by reusing, recycling, and repairing products.

Buying second hand furniture is a great way to support a circular economy because it keeps furniture out of landfills and gives it a new life. This not only reduces waste, but it also reduces the demand for new furniture, which can help to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and other environmental impacts associated with the furniture industry.

So, by buying second hand furniture, you're not only getting great deals on unique and stylish pieces, you're also supporting a more sustainable and circular economy.
