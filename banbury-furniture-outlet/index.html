Question 1: What factors should I consider when choosing garden furniture?

Answer 1: When selecting garden furniture, consider the following factors:

  • Purpose: Define how you'll use the space (relaxation, dining, entertaining).
  • Comfort: Choose pieces that offer ample support and comfort for extended periods.
  • Durability: Opt for furniture made from weather-resistant materials to withstand outdoor conditions.
  • Style: Pick pieces that complement your outdoor décor and personal taste.
  • Maintenance: Consider furniture that's easy to clean and maintain.

Question 7: How can I protect my garden furniture from harsh weather?

Answer 7: To protect your garden furniture from harsh weather:

  • Use Protective Covers: Invest in waterproof covers to shield furniture from rain, snow, and dust.
  • Store Cushions and Textiles: Keep cushions and textiles dry by storing them indoors when not in use.
  • Choose Fade-Resistant Fabrics: Opt for furniture with fade-resistant fabrics to prevent color fading from sun exposure.
  • Regular Cleaning: Regularly clean your furniture to remove dirt, debris, and stains that can damage the materials.
