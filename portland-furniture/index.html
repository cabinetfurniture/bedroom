Modern masterpieces

Portland is home to a thriving community of furniture designers and makers who are creating some of the most innovative and stylish modern furniture in the world. From sleek and minimalist pieces to bold and sculptural designs, there's something to suit every taste.

One of the best places to find modern masterpieces in Portland is at the annual Portland Modern Home Show. This event showcases the work of some of the city's top furniture designers and makers, and it's a great opportunity to see the latest trends in modern furniture.

If you're looking for something truly unique, you can also commission a custom piece from a local furniture maker. There are many talented artisans in Portland who can create one-of-a-kind pieces that are sure to become cherished family heirlooms.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your living room or a complete set of furniture for your new home, Portland is the perfect place to find modern masterpieces that will elevate your space.

In addition to the many furniture stores and showrooms in Portland, there are also a number of online retailers that sell modern furniture from Portland-based designers. This is a great option if you're looking for something specific or if you're on a budget.

Vintage finds

Portland is a treasure trove for vintage furniture lovers. With its many antique stores, thrift shops, and consignment shops, there's always something new and exciting to discover.

One of the best places to find vintage furniture in Portland is the Northwest Antique Mall. This massive mall has over 200 dealers selling everything from furniture to clothing to jewelry. You're sure to find something special here, whether you're looking for a mid-century modern credenza or a Victorian settee.

If you're on a budget, be sure to check out the city's many thrift stores. Goodwill and Salvation Army are always good places to start, but there are also a number of smaller, independent thrift stores that are worth exploring.

Another great way to find vintage furniture in Portland is to attend one of the city's many flea markets. The Portland Flea Market and the Alberta Street Flea Market are two of the most popular, and they both feature a wide variety of vintage furniture and other goods.

Whether you're looking for a one-of-a-kind piece or just a few small accents to add some vintage charm to your home, you're sure to find what you're looking for in Portland.

Rustic charm

Portland's love of the outdoors is reflected in its furniture style, with many pieces incorporating rustic charm. This look is characterized by natural materials, such as wood, leather, and stone, and simple, understated designs.

  • Reclaimed wood furniture

    Reclaimed wood furniture is a great way to add rustic charm to your home while being eco-friendly. This furniture is made from wood that has been reclaimed from old barns, warehouses, and other buildings. The wood is often distressed, which gives it a unique and character-filled look.

  • Live edge furniture

    Live edge furniture is another great way to bring the outdoors in. This furniture is made from wood that has been left with its natural edge, which creates a unique and organic look. Live edge furniture is often used to create tables, benches, and other statement pieces.

  • Antler furniture

    Antler furniture is a unique and eye-catching way to add rustic charm to your home. This furniture is made from the antlers of deer, elk, and other animals. Antler furniture is often used to create chandeliers, lamps, and other decorative pieces.

  • Wrought iron furniture

    Wrought iron furniture is a classic choice for rustic charm. This furniture is made from iron that has been heated and hammered into shape. Wrought iron furniture is often used to create beds, chairs, and other pieces with a timeless appeal.

Whether you're looking for a single piece to add a touch of rustic charm to your home or you're looking to furnish your entire home in this style, Portland has plenty of options to choose from.

Coastal vibes

Portland's proximity to the Pacific Ocean has a major influence on its furniture style. Many Portland furniture stores offer pieces that are inspired by the coastal lifestyle, with a focus on natural materials, light colors, and comfortable, relaxed designs.

One of the most popular coastal vibes furniture styles is the beach cottage style. This style is characterized by whitewashed wood, slipcovered furniture, and nautical accents. Beach cottage style furniture is perfect for creating a relaxed and inviting atmosphere in your home.

Another popular coastal vibes furniture style is the modern coastal style. This style is characterized by clean lines, neutral colors, and natural materials. Modern coastal style furniture is perfect for creating a sophisticated and stylish space that still has a relaxed, coastal feel.

No matter what your style, you're sure to find coastal vibes furniture in Portland that you'll love. Here are a few tips for incorporating coastal vibes into your home:

  • Use natural materials, such as wood, rattan, and jute.
  • Choose light colors, such as white, beige, and blue.
  • Add nautical accents, such as seashells, driftwood, and rope.
  • Create a relaxed and inviting atmosphere with comfortable furniture and plenty of natural light.

Scandinavian simplicity

Scandinavian design is known for its simplicity, functionality, and beauty. This style is characterized by clean lines, natural materials, and a focus on comfort. Scandinavian furniture is perfect for creating a modern and inviting space in your home.

One of the most popular Scandinavian furniture styles is the mid-century modern style. This style is characterized by its clean lines, tapered legs, and organic shapes. Mid-century modern furniture is often made from wood, leather, and other natural materials.

Another popular Scandinavian furniture style is the contemporary Scandinavian style. This style is characterized by its minimalist design and its use of neutral colors and natural materials. Contemporary Scandinavian furniture is often made from wood, metal, and glass.

No matter what your style, you're sure to find Scandinavian simplicity furniture in Portland that you'll love. Here are a few tips for incorporating Scandinavian simplicity into your home:

  • Use clean lines and simple shapes.
  • Choose natural materials, such as wood, leather, and wool.
  • Use neutral colors, such as white, black, and gray.
  • Add pops of color with textiles and accessories.
  • Create a cozy and inviting atmosphere with soft lighting and comfortable furniture.

Industrial edge

Portland's industrial heritage is reflected in its furniture style, with many pieces incorporating industrial edge. This look is characterized by raw materials, such as metal, wood, and concrete, and simple, functional designs.

  • Reclaimed metal furniture

This furniture is made from metal that has been reclaimed from old factories, warehouses, and other buildings. The metal is often distressed, which gives it a unique and character-filled look.

  • Concrete furniture

  • Industrial lighting

    Industrial lighting is a great way to add a touch of industrial edge to your home. This lighting is often made from metal or concrete and has a simple, functional design. Industrial lighting is perfect for creating a unique and stylish atmosphere in your home.

  • Vintage industrial furniture

    Vintage industrial furniture is a great way to add a touch of history to your home. This furniture is often made from metal or wood and has a simple, functional design. Vintage industrial furniture is perfect for creating a unique and character-filled look in your home.

Whether you're looking for a single piece to add a touch of industrial edge to your home or you're looking to furnish your entire home in this style, Portland has plenty of options to choose from.

Eco-friendly options

Portland is known for its commitment to sustainability, and this is reflected in its furniture scene. Many Portland furniture stores offer eco-friendly options, such as furniture made from recycled materials, sustainable wood, and low-VOC finishes.

One of the most popular eco-friendly furniture options in Portland is furniture made from recycled materials. This furniture is made from materials that would otherwise be thrown away, such as plastic bottles, cardboard, and wood scraps.

Another popular eco-friendly furniture option in Portland is furniture made from sustainable wood. This wood is harvested from forests that are managed in a sustainable way, which means that the forests are replanted and protected for future generations.

Finally, many Portland furniture stores offer furniture with low-VOC finishes. VOCs are volatile organic compounds that can be harmful to human health and the environment. Low-VOC finishes emit fewer VOCs, which makes them a healthier choice for your home.

Whether you're looking for a single piece to add a touch of eco-friendliness to your home or you're looking to furnish your entire home in this style, Portland has plenty of options to choose from.
