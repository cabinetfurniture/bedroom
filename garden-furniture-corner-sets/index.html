Consider the materials

The materials used to make your garden furniture corner set will have a big impact on its durability, comfort, and appearance. Here are a few of the most common materials used for garden furniture:

Teak is a durable hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot and decay. It is also a beautiful wood with a rich, golden-brown color. Teak furniture is more expensive than other types of garden furniture, but it will last for many years with proper care.

Eucalyptus is another durable hardwood that is a good choice for garden furniture. It is not as resistant to rot and decay as teak, but it is still a good choice for outdoor use. Eucalyptus furniture is typically less expensive than teak furniture.

Wicker is a type of woven material that is made from natural fibers, such as rattan or bamboo. Wicker furniture is lightweight and easy to move around, and it is also relatively inexpensive. However, wicker furniture is not as durable as teak or eucalyptus furniture, and it can be damaged by rain and sun.

Aluminum is a lightweight and durable metal that is often used for garden furniture. Aluminum furniture is rust-resistant and easy to care for, but it can be more expensive than other types of garden furniture.

Plastic is a versatile material that can be used to make a variety of different types of garden furniture. Plastic furniture is lightweight and inexpensive, but it is not as durable as other materials, such as teak or aluminum. Plastic furniture can also be damaged by UV rays, so it is important to choose a set that is designed for outdoor use.

Once you have considered the different materials used to make garden furniture corner sets, you can start to narrow down your choices. By choosing the right materials, you can be sure to find a set that will last for years to come and provide you with many years of enjoyment.
