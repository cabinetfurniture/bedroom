FAQ

Have questions about garden furniture? We've compiled a list of frequently asked questions and answers to help you make informed decisions and create an inviting outdoor space.

Question 1: What is the best material for garden furniture?
Answer 1: The choice of material for garden furniture depends on your specific needs and preferences. Popular options include teak, acacia, metal, and resin wicker. Teak and acacia are naturally durable and weather-resistant, while metal and resin wicker offer lightweight and low-maintenance alternatives.

Question 2: How can I protect my garden furniture from the elements?
Answer 2: To protect your garden furniture from sun, rain, and wind, consider using weather-resistant covers or storing it in a sheltered area when not in use. Regular cleaning and maintenance can also prolong its lifespan and keep it looking its best.

Question 3: What size garden furniture should I choose?
Answer 3: When selecting garden furniture, consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you typically entertain. Choose pieces that are proportionate to your space, leaving enough room for comfortable movement and activities.
