FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture to help you make an informed decision:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Garden furniture should be made from durable materials that can withstand the elements. Some popular choices include teak, acacia, and aluminum.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Consider the size of your garden and the number of people you typically entertain. Choose furniture that is proportionate to the space and allows for comfortable movement.

Question 3: What style of garden furniture should I choose?
Garden furniture comes in a variety of styles, from traditional to modern. Choose a style that complements your home and personal taste.

Question 4: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Regular cleaning and maintenance will help extend the life of your garden furniture. Clean it with a mild soap solution and protect it from the elements with a furniture cover.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Some garden furniture materials, such as teak and aluminum, are more resistant to the elements and can be left outside all year round. However, it's best to store cushions and other accessories indoors during harsh weather.

Question 6: How do I protect my garden furniture from fading?
Use a UV protectant spray or stain to help prevent your garden furniture from fading due to sun exposure.

By following these tips, you can choose and care for garden furniture that will provide years of enjoyment.
