Tips

Take your garden furniture care to the next level with these practical tips:

Tip 1: Clean before you polish: Before applying Fabulosa Furniture Polish, ensure your garden furniture is clean and free of dirt, dust, and debris. This will help the polish adhere better to the surface and provide optimal protection.

Tip 2: Apply in the shade: For best results, apply Fabulosa Furniture Polish in a shaded area. Direct sunlight can cause the polish to dry too quickly, making it difficult to work with and potentially leaving streaks.

Tip 3: Use a microfiber cloth: Opt for a soft, microfiber cloth when applying Fabulosa Furniture Polish. Microfiber cloths are gentle on furniture surfaces and won't leave behind any lint or residue.

Tip 4: Buff to a shine: After applying the polish, use a clean microfiber cloth to buff the surface of your furniture. This will remove any excess polish and bring out a brilliant shine.
