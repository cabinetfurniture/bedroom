Sustainable materials

At 9 Lives Furniture, we believe that sustainability is just as important as style and functionality. That's why we use sustainable materials in all of our products, from the wood in our frames to the fabric on our upholstery.

  • FSC-certified wood

    All of the wood we use in our frames is FSC-certified, which means that it comes from responsibly managed forests. FSC certification ensures that the forests are harvested in a way that minimizes environmental impact and protects biodiversity.

  • Recycled materials

    We use recycled materials whenever possible, such as recycled plastic and metal. This helps to reduce our environmental footprint and conserve natural resources.

  • Low-VOC finishes

    The finishes we use on our furniture are low in VOCs (volatile organic compounds). VOCs are harmful air pollutants that can contribute to respiratory problems and other health issues. Our low-VOC finishes help to create a healthier indoor environment for you and your family.

  • Sustainable fabrics

    We offer a wide range of sustainable fabrics for our upholstery, including organic cotton, hemp, and recycled polyester. These fabrics are not only durable and beautiful, but they also have a lower environmental impact than traditional fabrics.

By using sustainable materials, we can help to reduce our environmental footprint and create furniture that is both stylish and eco-friendly. We believe that everyone deserves to live in a beautiful and healthy home, and we are committed to doing our part to make that possible.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about garden furniture:

Question 1: What materials are best for garden furniture?
Answer:

The best materials for garden furniture are those that are durable and weather-resistant. Some of the most popular materials include teak, aluminum, and wicker.

Question 2: How do I choose the right size garden furniture?
Answer:

When choosing the right size garden furniture, it is important to consider the size of your outdoor space and the number of people you will be entertaining. You should also consider the size of your furniture in relation to the other elements in your outdoor space, such as your patio or deck.

Question 3: How do I care for my garden furniture?
Answer:

The best way to care for your garden furniture is to clean it regularly with a mild soap and water solution. You should also protect your furniture from the elements by covering it when it is not in use.

Question 4: How can I make my garden furniture more comfortable?
Answer:

There are a few things you can do to make your garden furniture more comfortable. One is to add cushions and pillows. Another is to use a throw blanket on cooler evenings.

Question 5: How can I style my garden furniture?
Answer:

There are many ways to style your garden furniture. One way is to add some plants and flowers. Another way is to use some colorful throw pillows and cushions.

Question 6: Where can I buy garden furniture?
