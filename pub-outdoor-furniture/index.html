The Ultimate Guide to Choosing the Perfect Pub Outdoor Furniture

Written by William Apr 24, 2025 · 9 min read

Creating a welcoming and inviting outdoor space for your pub is crucial to attracting and retaining customers. One of the most important elements of any outdoor area is the furniture. Choosing the right pub outdoor furniture can make all the difference in creating a comfortable and stylish space that will keep your patrons coming back for more.

In this comprehensive guide, we will delve into the various factors to consider when selecting pub outdoor furniture. We will cover everything from materials and durability to style and functionality. Whether you're just starting to plan your outdoor area or looking to upgrade your existing furniture, this guide will provide you with the information you need to make an informed decision.

Before we dive into the details of choosing pub outdoor furniture, let's first explore the different types of materials typically used in outdoor furniture.

Pub Outdoor Furniture

When choosing pub outdoor furniture, there are several important points to consider:

  • Durability
  • Comfort
  • Style
  • Functionality
  • Maintenance

By keeping these factors in mind, you can select furniture that will meet the specific needs of your pub and provide your customers with a comfortable and enjoyable outdoor experience.

Durability

When it comes to pub outdoor furniture, durability is of utmost importance. Your furniture will be exposed to the elements, including rain, sun, and wind. It will also be subjected to heavy use by patrons. Therefore, it is essential to choose furniture that is made from durable materials that can withstand these conditions.

Some of the most durable materials for pub outdoor furniture include:

  • Teak: Teak is a hardwood that is naturally resistant to rot, decay, and insects. It is also very strong and durable, making it an excellent choice for outdoor furniture.
  • Aluminum: Aluminum is a lightweight and corrosion-resistant metal that is perfect for outdoor use. It is also very easy to clean and maintain.
  • Polyethylene: Polyethylene is a synthetic material that is UV-resistant and weatherproof. It is also very durable and can withstand heavy use.

In addition to the material, the construction of the furniture also plays a role in its durability. Look for furniture that is well-made with strong joints and sturdy frames. Avoid furniture with flimsy construction or loose joints, as these are more likely to break or collapse.

By choosing durable pub outdoor furniture, you can ensure that your furniture will last for many years to come, providing your customers with a comfortable and enjoyable outdoor experience.

Comfort

Comfort is another important factor to consider when choosing pub outdoor furniture. Your customers should be able to relax and enjoy their time spent in your outdoor area. Therefore, it is important to choose furniture that is comfortable to sit in.

There are a few things to keep in mind when assessing the comfort of pub outdoor furniture:

  • Cushions: Cushions can make a big difference in the comfort of your furniture. Look for cushions that are made from high-quality materials and that are thick enough to provide good support. Avoid cushions that are too thin or that are made from cheap materials, as these will not be comfortable to sit on for long periods of time.
  • Back support: Back support is also important for comfort. Look for chairs and sofas with high backs that provide good support for your back and neck. Avoid chairs and sofas with low backs or no backs, as these will not be comfortable to sit in for long periods of time.
  • Armrests: Armrests can also add to the comfort of your furniture. Look for chairs and sofas with armrests that are wide enough to provide good support for your arms. Avoid chairs and sofas with narrow or no armrests, as these will not be as comfortable to sit in.

By choosing comfortable pub outdoor furniture, you can ensure that your customers will have a relaxing and enjoyable experience in your outdoor area.

Style

The style of your pub outdoor furniture should complement the overall look and feel of your pub. If you have a traditional pub, you may want to choose furniture with a classic design. If you have a more modern pub, you may want to choose furniture with a more contemporary design.
  • Classic: Classic pub outdoor furniture is typically made from wood or wrought iron and features traditional designs. This type of furniture is often seen in traditional pubs and restaurants.
  • Contemporary: Contemporary pub outdoor furniture is typically made from modern materials, such as aluminum or plastic, and features clean, modern lines. This type of furniture is often seen in more modern pubs and restaurants.
  • Transitional: Transitional pub outdoor furniture combines elements of both classic and contemporary design. This type of furniture is often seen in pubs and restaurants that have a mix of traditional and modern décor.
No matter what style of pub you have, it is important to choose furniture that is comfortable and durable. You should also choose furniture that is easy to clean and maintain.

Functionality

The functionality of your pub outdoor furniture is also important to consider. You will want to choose furniture that is easy to move around and that can be used for a variety of purposes.
  • Stackable: Stackable furniture is easy to move around and store when not in use. This type of furniture is ideal forpubs that have limited storage space.
  • Foldable:Foldable furniture is also easy to move around and store. This type of furniture is ideal forpubs that have limited space or that need to be able to quickly set up and take down their outdoor furniture.
  • Multi-PROBLE: Multi- purpose furniture can be used for a variety of purposes. For example, a bench can be used for sitting, as a table, or as a footrest.

Maintenance

The maintenance of your pub outdoor furniture is also important to consider. You will want to choose furniture that is easy to clean and maintain.
  • Weather-resistant: Weather-resistant furniture is designed to withstand the elements, including rain, sun, and wind. This type of furniture is ideal for pubs that have outdoor areas that are exposed to the elements.
  • Easy to clean: Easy-to-clean furniture is easy to wipe down and clean. This type of furniture is ideal for pubs that have high-traffic outdoor areas.
  • Low-maintenance: Low-maintenance furniture does not require a lot of maintenance. This type of furniture is ideal for pubs that do not have a lot of time or resources to spend on maintenance.
By choosing low-maintenance pub outdoor furniture, you can save time and money on maintenance and keep your furniture looking its best for longer.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you choose and care for your garden furniture:

Tip 1: Choose the right material. The material of your garden furniture will affect its durability, comfort, and style. Wood is a classic choice for garden furniture, but it requires more maintenance than other materials. Metal is a durable and low-maintenance option, but it can be uncomfortable in hot weather. Plastic is a lightweight and affordable option, but it is not as durable as wood or metal. Wicker is a natural material that is both stylish and comfortable, but it can be expensive.

Tip 2: Consider your needs. Before you start shopping for garden furniture, think about how you will be using it. If you plan on entertaining guests, you will need to choose furniture that is comfortable and stylish. If you have a small garden, you will need to choose furniture that is compact and easy to move around. If you have children, you will need to choose furniture that is durable and easy to clean.

Tip 3: Care for your furniture. To keep your garden furniture looking its best, it is important to clean it regularly. You can use a mild soap and water solution to clean most types of garden furniture. Be sure to rinse the furniture thoroughly after cleaning it. You should also store your furniture in a dry place during the winter months.

Tip 4: Accessorize your furniture. You can add personality to your garden furniture by adding accessories, such as cushions, pillows, and throws. You can also add a touch of greenery by adding plants and flowers to your outdoor space.

By following these tips, you can choose and care for garden furniture that will last for many years to come.
