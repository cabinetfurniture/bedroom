Measure your cushions before you start shopping to ensure a snug fit.

One of the most important steps when shopping for replacement rattan furniture cushion covers is to measure your existing cushions. This will help you ensure that you choose the right size covers that will fit snugly and prevent the cushions from shifting around.

To measure your cushions, you will need a measuring tape. Start by measuring the length of the cushion from one end to the other. Then, measure the width of the cushion from one side to the other. Finally, measure the height of the cushion from the top to the bottom.

Once you have the measurements of your cushions, you can start shopping for replacement covers. Be sure to compare the measurements of your cushions to the measurements of the covers to ensure a snug fit.

If you are unsure about the size of replacement covers to order, it is always better to err on the side of caution and order a size larger. You can always have the covers taken in by a seamstress if they are too large.

By following these tips, you can ensure that you choose the right size replacement rattan furniture cushion covers for your needs.
