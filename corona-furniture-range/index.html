Sustainable Materials

The Corona furniture range is committed to sustainability, utilizing materials that are both durable and eco-friendly.

  • FSC-Certified Wood:

    The primary material used in the Corona range is FSC-certified wood, ensuring that it comes from responsibly managed forests. This certification guarantees that the wood is harvested in a way that minimizes environmental impact and promotes the long-term health of the forests.

  • Rattan and Bamboo:

    Rattan and bamboo are two rapidly renewable resources that are used extensively in the Corona range. These materials are known for their strength, durability, and natural beauty, making them ideal for furniture construction.

  • Recycled Metals:

    Various pieces in the Corona range incorporate recycled metals, such as aluminum and iron. This not only reduces the environmental impact of the furniture but also adds a unique industrial touch to the designs.

  • Water-Based Finishes:

    The Corona range is finished with water-based stains and lacquers, which emit fewer harmful VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds) compared to traditional finishes. This makes the furniture safer for both indoor air quality and the environment.

By using sustainable materials, the Corona furniture range not only creates beautiful and long-lasting pieces but also contributes to a greener future.

FAQ

If you have questions about choosing, maintaining, or enjoying your garden furniture, check out our frequently asked questions below:

Question 1: How do I choose the right garden furniture for my needs?
Answer 1: Consider factors like the size of your garden, the intended use (dining, lounging, etc.), your personal style, and the durability of the materials.

Question 2: What are the most durable materials for garden furniture?
Answer 2: Teak, aluminum, and weather-resistant wicker are great choices for their durability and resistance to the elements.

Question 3: How can I protect my garden furniture from harsh weather?
Answer 3: Use furniture covers, store cushions and pillows indoors when not in use, and apply protective sealants regularly.

Question 4: How do I maintain my garden furniture?
Answer 4: Clean regularly with mild soap and water, tighten screws and bolts periodically, and consider re-staining or painting wooden furniture as needed.

Question 5: Can I leave my garden furniture outside all year round?
Answer 5: It depends on the materials and climate. Some furniture is designed to withstand outdoor conditions, but it's generally better to store cushions and pillows indoors and cover the furniture during harsh weather.

Question 6: How do I create a cozy and inviting outdoor space with my garden furniture?
Answer 6: Add comfortable cushions and throws, incorporate plants and flowers, use ambient lighting, and arrange the furniture to create conversation areas.

Question 7: Where can I find high-quality garden furniture at affordable prices?
Answer 7: Check online retailers, local furniture stores, and home improvement centers for a wide range of options to suit your budget.

Closing Paragraph for FAQ: These are just a few of the frequently asked questions about garden furniture. If you have any further questions, don't hesitate to consult with a furniture specialist or conduct further research online.

Tips

Follow these practical tips to choose, arrange, and maintain your garden furniture, creating an inviting outdoor space for relaxation and enjoyment:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Materials:
Consider the climate, intended use, and your personal style when selecting materials. Teak, aluminum, and weather-resistant wicker are durable and low-maintenance options.

Tip 2: Arrange for Comfort and Conversation:
Position your furniture to create comfortable seating areas that encourage conversation. Leave enough space between pieces for easy movement and access.

Tip 3: Add Comfort and Style with Cushions and Pillows:
Accessorize your garden furniture with comfortable cushions and pillows in weather-resistant fabrics. Choose colors and patterns that complement your outdoor décor.

Tip 4: Protect Your Furniture from the Elements:
Use furniture covers or store cushions and pillows indoors when not in use. Apply protective sealants or paints to wooden furniture regularly to shield against moisture and UV rays.

Tip 5: Incorporate Plants and Flowers:
Surround your garden furniture with potted plants, hanging baskets, or flower beds. Adding greenery creates a more inviting and relaxing atmosphere.

Closing Paragraph for Tips:
By following these tips, you can create a stylish and comfortable outdoor oasis that extends your living space and allows you to enjoy your garden to the fullest.

As you consider these tips, remember to prioritize quality, comfort, and personal style when selecting and arranging your garden furniture. With proper care and maintenance, your outdoor space will become a cherished retreat for relaxation and entertainment.
